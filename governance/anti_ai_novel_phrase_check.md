# Anti-AI Novel Phrase Check

Use this during drafting, revision, and chapter review.

This check targets generic AI novel phrasing: abstract emotional labels, fake suspense, polished summary endings, and compressed setting labels.

## Core Rule

A line can be fluent and still fail if it replaces action, object, choice, consequence, or relationship movement with a polished summary.

Openings and chapter endings should usually land on:

- action;
- object;
- bodily sensation tied to pressure;
- unfinished choice;
- concrete consequence;
- changed relationship;
- visible cost;
- abnormal evidence.

## 1. Emotion Labels

Suspicious examples:

```text
心跳漏了一拍
心里一紧
说不清是什么感觉
有那么一瞬间
他忽然有些恍惚
她不知道为什么难过
```

These are not always banned, but they cannot carry the main emotional turn.

Replace with:

- action under pressure;
- practical speech;
- hesitation;
- wrong choice;
- object handling;
- visible reaction from another character.

## 2. Summary Endings

Usually revise:

```text
这不是结束，而是开始
真正的问题才刚刚浮出水面
他终于明白
这一次，不一样了
一切都变了
命运从这一刻开始转向
```

Replace with concrete consequence or changed state.

## 3. Fake Suspense Lines

Usually revise:

```text
没人知道，命运的齿轮已经转动
他还不知道，危险正在靠近
黑暗中，有什么正在苏醒
故事才刚刚开始
```

Suspense should come from information, pressure, cost, or threat, not narrator warning.

## 4. Abstract Relationship Lines

Usually revise:

```text
两人的关系发生了微妙变化
空气安静下来
沉默说明了一切
他们之间多了一层说不清的东西
```

Replace with a specific changed behavior, withheld line, avoided gaze, practical concession, new refusal, or altered power relation.

## 5. Generic Realization Lines

Usually revise:

```text
答案一直在他心里
他真正要面对的是自己
原来最难的不是敌人，而是自己
她终于懂了什么叫选择
```

A realization must be proven by a different action, not announced.

## 6. Compressed Setting Labels

Usually revise:

```text
旧日规则
命运缝隙
秩序背面
时代残响
被遗忘的真相
城市的另一面
```

If a phrase compresses a world rule, institution, mystery, or theme, unpack it into ordinary scene-level facts.

## 7. AI-Polished Contrast Lines

Usually revise:

```text
不是因为害怕，而是因为明白
他失去的不是机会，而是退路
这不是奖励，是代价
她看见的不是门，是选择
```

Contrast lines are allowed only when they arise naturally from concrete action and are not doing the chapter's main meaning work.

## Review Rules

Mark `REVISE` if:

- more than one suspicious phrase appears in a short passage;
- a key payoff, emotional turn, or ending depends on a suspicious phrase;
- the line sounds like a narrator summarizing the theme instead of a character living the pressure;
- the phrase can be replaced by action, object, consequence, or ordinary speech without losing meaning;
- the opening or ending lands on a generic summary instead of a concrete memory point.
