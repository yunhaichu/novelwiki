# Reader Reward Check

Use this during chapter planning and review.

## Checklist

For each chapter, identify:

```text
Reader expectation:
Obstacle:
Protagonist action:
Visible gain:
Witness or reaction:
Next possibility:
```

## Rules

The chapter should not only show process.

The protagonist should make a visible gain, even if the gain is small.

The gain should affect position, access, information, treatment, relationship, or next-step possibility.

A hidden clue is useful, but it should not be the only reward.

If no one reacts and nothing changes on the page, the reward may be too weak.

## Revise If

- the chapter only adds hardship
- the protagonist gains nothing visible
- the gain is only private thought
- the chapter feels like a process log
- the ending creates no clear next possibility
