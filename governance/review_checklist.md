# Review Checklist

Use this checklist after a chapter draft is written and before any wiki update is created.

## Order

1. Read the chapter draft.
2. Check against the novel wiki.
3. Decide whether the chapter is allowed or needs revision.
4. Only after approval, create a wiki update draft.

## Checks

### Main Line

- Can the chapter be summarized as: protagonist wants X, but Y blocks it, so they try Z, resulting in W?
- Is there only one core drive?
- Is the current trigger clear?
- Does the ending create a concrete reason to continue?

### Wiki Consistency

- Does the chapter conflict with confirmed facts?
- Does it change a character's appearance, clothing, items, speech style, or behavior without cause?
- Does it reveal or explain unresolved secrets too early?

### Knowledge Boundary

- Does any character know information they should not reasonably know?
- Does the narration confirm something that should only be rumor, belief, inference, or partial observation?
- Does the wiki update turn a character's guess, rumor, or suspicion into confirmed fact?
- Are observed facts, hearsay, inference, and unknowns clearly separated?
- If a fact is only known through a character's words, record it as that character's claim, not as objective canon.

### Character

- Does each major character speak like themselves?
- Does each major character act according to their current pressure and desire?
- Does the protagonist stay within known ability boundaries?

### Style

- Is humor moderate?
- Does humor fit the scene?
- Are there strange lines that do not fit the speaker or situation?
- Is the narration too technical, too explanatory, or too modern?

### World

- Does the world still match the project direction?
- Does the chapter avoid simple good-versus-evil framing unless the novel wiki says otherwise?
- Does the chapter avoid fixed conflict patterns such as paperwork, blame, signing, custody, or record disputes unless explicitly required?

## Output

Return one of:

- ALLOW
- REVISE

If REVISE, list only the most important fixes.
