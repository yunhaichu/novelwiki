# Project

## Direction

This is a cultivation-world novel.

The world is ruled by sects and cultivation clans rather than traditional states. There is no simple righteous-versus-demonic divide. Strong factions have violent histories and hidden costs.

## Protagonist

Name: Zhou Ping.

Zhou Ping is born in this world. He is not a transmigrator.

He grew up near Qingheng Sect and has wanted to join the sect since childhood. His aptitude is ordinary, so he can only begin as a servant probationer.

## Core Focus

The early story should focus on:

- low-status entry into a powerful sect
- servant life as the lower edge of the cultivation system
- the gap between childhood imagination and real sect operation
- Zhou Ping moving from survival toward a real but difficult path of cultivation

## First Arc Engine

The first arc is driven by the three-month probation period.

Zhou Ping must draw qi into the body within three months, or he will be sent back down the mountain.

The daily qi-guiding lesson is only a short window of access, not a generous teaching system.

This creates pressure around:

- time
- position
- chores
- literacy
- attention from low-level staff
- access to older probationers or servants
- small resources that affect learning

## Direction Guardrail

Strange or dangerous events may appear, but they must support the cultivation-world story.

They should not become isolated puzzle events.

The story should not remain only a sequence of servant tasks.

Each chapter should connect at least one concrete event to Qingheng Sect's hierarchy, cultivation access, resource distribution, selection pressure, or status change.

## Early Arc Targets

Within the next few chapters, establish:

- what the qi-guiding lesson looks like
- why one short lesson is not enough for most probationers
- what older probationers or low-level servants know that newcomers do not
- how work assignments affect access to learning
- how Zhou Ping's small skills help, but do not let him solve everything cleanly
- what practical cost Zhou Ping pays for each small step upward
