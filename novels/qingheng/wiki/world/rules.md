# World Rules

## Confirmed Setting

The world is dominated by sects and cultivation clans.

There is no traditional state-centered order as the main structure.

There is no simple righteous-versus-demonic divide.

Strong factions have violent histories and hidden costs.

## Qingheng Sect

Qingheng Sect is a nearby powerful sect.

Young people from nearby towns and villages want to enter it because it is visible, powerful, and close.

The sect accepts people into different levels depending on aptitude, resources, and usefulness.

## Servant Disciples

Servant disciples are low-status members at the edge of the sect system.

Servant work should connect to the sect's larger cultivation operation, resources, infrastructure, hierarchy, or selection pressure.

Servant work should not become endless isolated chores.

## Unknown

Do not fully explain Qingheng Sect's internal structure too early.

Do not define all cultivation rules before the protagonist has reason to learn them.
