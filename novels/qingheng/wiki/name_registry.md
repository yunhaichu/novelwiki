# Name Registry

## Naming Style

Names should be simple, natural, and easy to remember.

Avoid overly literary, cold, rare, or obviously protagonist-like names.

## Approved Major Names

- Li Cheng: protagonist.

## Used Names

- Li Cheng
- Zhou Xiaoman
- Qingheng Sect

## Stable Role Labels

These roles are not yet personally named, but their labels must remain stable until renamed with approval:

- Old Liu
- green-robed youth
- fat steward
- round-faced boy

## Avoid Reusing

- Shen Yan
- Chen Yan
- Su Qing
- names using overly literary or cold aesthetic characters unless explicitly approved

## Notes

Major names should remain stable once approved.

Unnamed recurring roles should use stable labels until the story gives them names.
