# Name Registry

## Naming Style

Names should be simple, natural, and easy to remember.

Avoid overly literary, cold, rare, or obviously protagonist-like names.

## Approved Major Names

- Zhou Ping: protagonist.

## Used Names

- Zhou Ping
- Qingheng Sect

## Avoid Reusing

- Li Cheng
- Shen Yan
- Chen Yan
- Su Qing
- names using overly literary or cold aesthetic characters unless explicitly approved

## Notes

Major names should remain stable once approved.
