# Arc 001 After Golden Three Roadmap

## Status

Golden three review result: PASS with follow-up constraints.

The story may proceed beyond the opening, but it must not return to ordinary daily-task progression.

## Current Opening Outcome

After chapter 3:

- Zhou Ping is still an unmarked servant probationer.
- Zhou Ping has no true qi sense.
- Zhao Steward's register now contains multiple marks beside Zhou Ping's name.
- Wang Man feels left behind and resentful, but still wants to stay.
- Liu Er has noticed Zhou Ping's abnormal treatment and may become a small friction point.
- Liu Shun has given a limited practical warning but is not a mentor.
- Dirty work has helped Zhou Ping gain access, but it risks becoming a label or trap.

## Next Arc Function

The next short arc should convert the golden-three hook into first-month pressure.

It must show that:

```text
Being noticed by the register has consequences.
Those consequences are useful, but not clean rewards.
Zhou Ping must move beyond dirty work as his only path.
Wang Man's pressure cannot be ignored.
```

## Core Reader Reward Target

The reader wants to see:

```text
Zhou Ping's small register marks begin to matter on the page.
```

This must be visible through assignment, treatment, reaction, warning, status pressure, or access.

## Chapter 004 Target: Register Consequence

Purpose:

Turn the register marks into a visible consequence.

Reader expectation:

```text
The system has marked Zhou Ping. Now that mark should change something.
```

Required elements:

- Zhao Steward or another low-level staff member changes how Zhou Ping is handled.
- The change is visible to Wang Man and at least one other probationer.
- The change is not pure reward. It should give access while adding burden.
- Wang Man reacts; he should not reset into a normal companion.
- Liu Er or another peer should show that others now notice Zhou Ping's abnormal treatment.

Possible payoff:

- Zhou Ping is sent early to handle a count, record, delivery, or timing task related to servant work.
- This lets him arrive earlier or hear a small useful part of instruction.
- Someone else loses a slot, gets worse work, or changes tone after seeing him used differently.

Possible cost:

- Zhou Ping is now treated as someone who can be used for unpleasant or precise tasks.
- Wang Man feels excluded.
- Other probationers suspect he is currying favor.

Do not fully explain the register marks yet.

## Chapter 005 Target: Trade Instead of Dirt

Purpose:

Move beyond dirty work as the only crack.

Reader expectation:

```text
Zhou Ping should find another usable route: trade, numbers, timing, silence, or memory.
```

Required elements:

- Zhou Ping uses limited number literacy or observation to exchange for something.
- The exchange must have cost or risk.
- Wang Man should either be included at cost or excluded with emotional consequence.
- Liu Shun may provide a fragment only if there is a reason: trade, debt, silence, or shared risk.

Possible payoff:

- Zhou Ping receives a more exact phrase from the qi-guiding lesson.
- Zhou Ping learns which line position matters most.
- Zhou Ping gains a way to avoid one false response.

Possible cost:

- He owes someone.
- Wang Man feels used or left behind.
- Liu Er learns something about his method.

## Chapter 006 Target: First Month Pressure Appears

Purpose:

Widen the story from day-by-day survival to first-month elimination pressure.

Required elements:

- A first-month check, reshuffle, or token-mark rule becomes visible.
- Zhou Ping's register marks may help or hurt him.
- Wang Man's risk becomes sharper.
- Another probationer may fail or be clearly near failure.

Possible payoff:

- Zhou Ping is not promoted, but his path becomes more concrete.
- He gains a small advantage in the first-month check.

Possible cost:

- His advantage draws resentment or exploitation.
- Wang Man may be at risk of falling behind.

## Relationship Track

### Zhou Ping and Wang Man

Next chapters must maintain the relationship thorn.

Wang Man should not become only loyal or only resentful.

He wants to stay. He may envy Zhou Ping, but he also may need him.

A useful pattern:

```text
Wang Man resents being left behind.
Zhou Ping tries to help but cannot slow the system.
Wang Man must choose whether to pay a cost himself.
```

### Zhou Ping and Liu Shun

Liu Shun should remain useful but limited.

He should not explain systems for free.

He may trade a sentence for silence, work, or a future favor.

### Zhou Ping and Zhao Steward

Zhao Steward should remain observant, not warm.

His register marks should affect events before they are explained.

## Prohibited Drift

- Do not write another chapter that is only a new unpleasant task.
- Do not make dirty work the only path upward.
- Do not explain the register system too early.
- Do not resolve Wang Man's resentment too quickly.
- Do not let Zhou Ping gain true qi sense before the false/real distinction has more weight.
- Do not make Liu Shun a teacher.
- Do not make Zhao Steward kind or chatty.
