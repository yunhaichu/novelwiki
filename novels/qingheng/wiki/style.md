# Style

## Default Style

- clear main line
- grounded cultivation world
- light humor in moderation
- no modern terms
- no transmigration framing
- no pure technical explanation
- no overuse of jokes

## Humor Rules

Humor should come from:

- character reactions
- social mismatch
- sect rules that feel cold or absurd
- small misunderstandings
- ordinary people facing harsh systems

Humor should not erase danger.

## Tone Rules

Qingheng Sect should feel powerful, old, and unsafe.

The sect is not a righteous sanctuary. It is a strong faction with its own rules and history.

Do not explain everything too early.

## Language Rules

Character speech should match status, age, fear, and situation.

Avoid overly literary names and overly poetic dialogue.

Avoid author-like jokes that do not fit the speaker.
