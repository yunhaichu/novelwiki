# Fat Steward

## Basic Info

Name: unknown.

Stable label: fat steward.

Role: Qingheng Sect servant registration steward.

## Current Function

Registers servant disciples and assigns initial lodging.

Represents the sect's practical, cold use of low-status recruits.

## Speech Style

- smiling but not warm
- practical
- brief
- uses plain words that make danger feel routine

## Knowledge Boundary

He knows registration information and servant procedures.

He does not need to explain deeper sect secrets.

## Do Not Write

- Do not make him a comic fool.
- Do not make him openly villainous in a simple way.
- Do not make him explain all servant rules too early.
