# Liu Shun

## Basic Info

Name: Liu Shun.

Identity: servant probationer with one mark on his wood token.

Current role: older probationer in the East Shed.

## Function

Liu Shun shows the information gap between newcomers and probationers who have already survived some time.

He is not a formal teacher or mentor.

## Desire

He wants to survive probation and keep small advantages for himself.

## Speech Style

- plain
- practical
- not warm
- says only what is useful or what he is willing to trade

## Knowledge Boundary

He knows some probation habits, work assignment patterns, and practical survival details from experience.

He does not know high-level sect secrets.

## Do Not Write

- do not make him a hidden master
- do not make him generously explain everything
- do not make him a pure villain
- do not let him solve Zhou Ping's problem for free
