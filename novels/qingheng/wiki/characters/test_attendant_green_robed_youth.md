# Green-robed Youth

## Basic Info

Name: unknown.

Stable label: green-robed youth.

Role: Qingheng Sect recruitment examiner at the spiritual-root table.

## Current Function

He measures spiritual roots and decides whether a recruit can enter the outer sect, servant path, or another recruitment track.

## Speech Style

- brief
- flat
- low emotion
- does not comfort recruits
- speaks from procedure and rank

## Knowledge Boundary

He can know the test result shown by the jade plate.

He can know Qingheng Sect's recruitment rules.

He should not know a recruit's family business unless a register, attendant, or the recruit provides that information.

## Do Not Write

- Do not make him explain the whole recruitment system.
- Do not make him know personal background without source.
- Do not make him sympathetic unless later text creates a reason.
