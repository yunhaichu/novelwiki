# Zhao Steward

## Basic Info

Name: Zhao Steward.

Role: Qingheng Sect recruitment and servant registration steward.

## Function

Zhao Steward represents the practical side of the sect's lower administration.

He registers low-status recruits and explains only the minimum necessary rules.

## Speech Style

- plain
- brief
- practical
- not warm, but not exaggeratedly cruel

## Knowledge Boundary

He knows recruitment records, servant registration rules, and basic servant procedures.

He does not need to explain deeper sect secrets.

## Do Not Write

- do not make him a simple villain
- do not make him a comic fool
- do not make him explain the entire sect system
