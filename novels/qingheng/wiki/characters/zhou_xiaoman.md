# Zhou Xiaoman

## Basic Info

Name: Zhou Xiaoman.

Origin: east-town rice shop family.

Status: same recruitment batch as Li Cheng.

## Current Function

Zhou Xiaoman shows that Qingheng Sect accepts people not only by aptitude, but also by resources.

## Known Facts

- He has mixed spiritual roots.
- His family has enough resources to be considered for a registered disciple path.
- He does not enter the servant path in chapter 001.

## Knowledge Boundary

Other characters should not know his family business unless he says it, a register shows it, or someone from the same area recognizes him.

## Speech Style

- anxious
- talks more than Li Cheng
- worries about whether he can enter the outer sect
- practical fears, such as being sent back to family work

## Do Not Write

- Do not make him suddenly brave.
- Do not make him a pure joke character.
- Do not let unrelated characters know his family background without a source.
