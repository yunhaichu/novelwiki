# Round-faced Boy

## Basic Info

Name: unknown.

Stable label: round-faced boy.

Status: new servant disciple in the same batch as Li Cheng.

Current location: East Shed.

## Current Function

He provides ordinary servant-disciple reactions: fear, confusion, nervous questions, and small low-pressure humor.

## Speech Style

- more talkative than Li Cheng
- asks practical questions
- speaks in a low voice when afraid
- can provide light humor, but should not become a pure comic role

## Knowledge Boundary

He does not understand Qingheng Sect's night rules.

He only knows rumors or what he has personally seen.

## Do Not Write

- Do not make him suddenly knowledgeable about sect secrets.
- Do not make him fearless without cause.
- Do not use him for constant jokes.
