# Liu Er

## Basic Info

Name: Liu Er.

Identity: new servant probationer in the same early batch as Zhou Ping and Wang Man.

Current role: peer friction character.

## Function

Liu Er shows how other probationers respond when Zhou Ping receives unusual attention from Zhao Steward.

He should not be only a repeating mockery machine.

## Desire

He wants to avoid the worst tasks and does not want another new probationer to gain an advantage over him.

## Pressure

He has already seen Zhou Ping turn unwanted work and register attention into visible access.

This makes him watch, copy, joke, and test boundaries.

## Surface Behavior

- jokes lightly when uneasy
- watches where Zhou Ping stands
- copies useful moves while pretending it is accidental
- speaks to nearby people rather than confronting Zhou Ping directly
- frames Zhou Ping's treatment as suspicious without fully explaining his own worry

## Hidden Pressure

- fears falling behind another new probationer
- fears choosing wrong work or wrong position
- wants to learn Zhou Ping's method without asking for help
- wants others to notice Zhou Ping's advantage so pressure does not fall only on Liu Er

## Will Say

- "你们站这边？那我也透透风。"
- "我就随便站站。"
- "这地方又不是你家的。"
- "管事今天没叫你？"
- "我听别人说的。"

## Will Not Say

- "我嫉妒你。"
- "我怕你比我先留下。"
- "我想学你的方法。"
- "我就是想坏你的事。"

## Stress Actions

- shifts closer after seeing Zhou Ping choose a position
- repeats rumors in a joking tone
- stops mocking when Zhao Steward looks over
- watches objects in Zhou Ping's hand before speaking
- moves early next time without announcing why

## Speech Style

- short
- casual
- lightly provocative
- should not become a villain speech pattern
- should not explain rules for the reader

## Knowledge Boundary

He knows only what he sees, hears from peers, or experiences directly.

He does not know Zhao Steward's real criteria or the meaning of register marks.

## Do Not Write

- do not make him a stock bully
- do not make him insult every chapter
- do not make him state his jealousy directly
- do not let him know hidden systems
- do not make him fail every time for easy payoff
