# Wang Man

## Basic Info

Name: Wang Man.

Identity: village boy from the same recruitment batch as Zhou Ping.

Current role: peer character in the entry arc.

## Function

Wang Man shows an ordinary recruit's fear, expectations, and practical concerns.

He is not a pure comic role.

## Desire

He wants to enter Qingheng Sect, but he also worries about food, work, and being sent home.

## Speech Style

- plain
- a little talkative
- asks practical questions
- nervous humor is allowed but should not dominate

## Knowledge Boundary

He knows only village-level information, rumors, and what he directly sees during recruitment.

## Do Not Write

- do not make him know sect secrets
- do not make him fearless
- do not use him for constant jokes
