# Wang Man

## Basic Info

Name: Wang Man.

Identity: village boy from the same recruitment batch as Zhou Ping.

Current role: peer character in the entry arc.

## Function

Wang Man shows an ordinary recruit's fear, expectations, practical concerns, and social comparison with Zhou Ping.

He is not a pure comic role.

## Desire

He wants to enter Qingheng Sect, but he also worries about food, work, being sent home, and disappointing his family.

## Pressure

Wang Man is less controlled than Zhou Ping. He reacts faster, worries aloud, and uses small talk to cover fear.

His worries should reveal real pressure, not just produce jokes.

## Speech Style

- plain
- a little talkative
- asks practical questions
- nervous humor is allowed but should not dominate
- repeated food jokes or object jokes should be limited

## Knowledge Boundary

He knows only village-level information, rumors, and what he directly sees during recruitment.

## Do Not Write

- do not make him know sect secrets
- do not make him fearless
- do not use him for constant jokes
- do not reduce him to one recurring object, habit, or gag
- do not make every line of his dialogue a punchline
