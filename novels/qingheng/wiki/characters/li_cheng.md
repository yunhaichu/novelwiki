# Li Cheng

## Basic Info

Name: Li Cheng.

Identity: village boy, new servant disciple of Qingheng Sect.

Origin: grew up near Qingheng Sect.

Aptitude: ordinary. Enters Qingheng Sect as a servant disciple rather than an outer disciple.

## Character Core

Li Cheng has wanted to enter Qingheng Sect since childhood.

He is not hot-blooded in a loud way. He watches first, speaks little, and tries not to look afraid.

He knows strong sects are not clean, but still wants to enter because the sect is near, visible, and powerful.

## Speech Style

- short sentences
- plain words
- rarely explains too much
- sometimes dry responses
- not poetic
- not modern

## Behavior Style

- observes small details
- avoids unnecessary attention
- does not challenge rules too early
- acts carefully under pressure
- can endure discomfort

## Weaknesses

- ordinary aptitude
- little knowledge of real sect life
- underestimates how dangerous servant work is
- wants to stay even when signs are bad

## Do Not Write

- do not make him a transmigrator
- do not give him modern knowledge
- do not make him suddenly understand complex sect secrets
- do not make him a loud heroic speaker
- do not make every judgment correct
