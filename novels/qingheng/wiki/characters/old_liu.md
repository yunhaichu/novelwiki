# Old Liu

## Basic Info

Role: cart driver from Li Cheng's village area.

Status: appears in chapter 001 as the person who brings young Li Cheng near Qingheng Sect's mountain gate.

## Function

Old Liu gives Li Cheng an early view of Qingheng Sect's power and violence.

## Knowledge Boundary

Old Liu's statements about the sword marks are hearsay or local telling unless later confirmed.

He does not define objective canon by himself.

## Speech Style

- plain
- brief
- practical
- speaks like someone used to roads, carts, and local stories

## Confirmed Claims

- He says the sword marks on Qingheng Sect's gate were left by Liu family sword cultivators.
- He says Qingheng Sect kept the marks to show others that the Liu family came and died there.

## Do Not Write

- Do not treat his claims as confirmed truth without later evidence.
- Do not make him an all-knowing guide.
