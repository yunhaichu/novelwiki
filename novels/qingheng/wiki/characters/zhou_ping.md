# Zhou Ping

## Basic Info

Name: Zhou Ping.

Identity: local village boy, future servant disciple candidate of Qingheng Sect.

Origin: grew up near Qingheng Sect.

Aptitude: ordinary.

## Character Core

Zhou Ping has wanted to join Qingheng Sect since childhood.

He is not a transmigrator and has no modern knowledge.

He should feel like a normal local boy: cautious, hopeful, inexperienced, and sometimes wrong.

## Desire

He wants to leave the limits of village life and enter the larger world represented by Qingheng Sect.

## Weaknesses

- ordinary aptitude
- limited knowledge of real sect life
- likely to misread sect danger at first
- can be afraid, embarrassed, and wrong

## Speech Style

- plain
- simple
- not poetic
- not overly clever
- fewer polished aphorisms

## Behavior Style

- observes, but not perfectly
- tries to endure pressure
- may hesitate or choose poorly
- should learn from consequence rather than always solving problems correctly

## Do Not Write

- do not make him a transmigrator
- do not give him modern knowledge
- do not make him perfectly calm under danger
- do not make him solve every problem correctly
- do not make him speak like a mature strategist
