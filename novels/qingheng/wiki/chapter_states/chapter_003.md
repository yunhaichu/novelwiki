## Chapter Summary

After Zhou Ping's visible gain in chapter 2, Wang Man feels left behind and asks why Zhou Ping did not call him earlier. The next morning Zhou Ping tries to include Wang Man, but Wang Man hesitates over the dirty work and loses the chance. Zhao Steward assigns Zhou Ping to kitchen-back ditch cleaning, while Wang Man is sent to the lower spring again. Zhou Ping performs the dirtier work and receives a new task from the kitchen-back worker: remember and deliver changed bucket counts. He brings the count tokens and numbers to Zhao Steward before the qi-guiding lesson, drawing visible attention. During the lesson Zhao Steward teaches about false sensations. Zhou Ping feels heat but does not chase it, while another probationer does and is corrected. After the lesson Wang Man confronts Zhou Ping. Zhao Steward adds a short stroke beside Zhou Ping's name, making the register marks more visible but still unexplained.
