## Review Result

ALLOW as post-golden-three chapter 4 after glossary rewrite.

## Arc Function

Chapter 4 serves: Register Consequence.

It turns Zhao Steward's marks in the register from hidden hook into visible treatment change, while keeping the meaning of the marks unexplained.

## Chapter Summary

After the golden-three opening, Zhou Ping expects to return to dirty work behind the kitchen, but Zhao Steward calls him early before ordinary new-probationer assignment and sends him to prepare the incense used for the qi-guiding lesson. This creates a visible change: Zhou Ping is handled differently because Zhao Steward has already written marks beside his name. The task is not a clean reward. If the red line on the incense is tied wrong, the whole lesson may become shorter or inaccurate and others may blame him. Liu Shun warns him that the task is useful but risky. During the lesson, the incense burns correctly, Zhao Steward says the timing is accurate, and others hear it. Wang Man asks Zhou Ping to call him next time if another chance appears, while Liu Er suggests that Zhao Steward's register already treats Zhou Ping differently. Zhou Ping still has no mark on the back of his gray wood token, but others now know Zhao Steward's register has his name and marks.
