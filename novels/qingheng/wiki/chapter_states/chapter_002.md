# Chapter 002 State

## Review Result

ALLOW after revision.

## Chapter Summary

Li Cheng wants to complete his first servant assignment after the midnight disturbance, but the paving task hides abnormal bricks, uneven ground, and a cracked step near the pine tree, so he tries to inspect the ground and adjust the bricks before laying them, resulting in a dark old brick briefly lighting up and the fat steward warning him not to sleep too deeply that night.

## Core Drive

Li Cheng wants to survive and complete the first day's servant work without losing food or attracting unnecessary trouble.

## Current Trigger

The new servant disciples are woken at yin hour and assigned work in the servant yard after the first night's midnight bells and dragging sound.

## Core Obstacle

The paving task appears ordinary but includes cold gray bricks, uneven ground, direction-sensitive markings, a cracked section near the pine tree, and darker old bricks with red traces in their markings.

## Local Progress

Li Cheng notices that the ground under the line is uneven, that some areas sound hollow, and that turning the brick markings changes the cold vapor. He fills the hollow crack before placing a darker old brick.

## New Trouble

When the darker old brick is placed, its red marking briefly lights and then fades. The fat steward tells Li Cheng not to sleep too deeply that night.

## Ending Hook

Li Cheng connects the daytime paving task with the previous night's dragging sound as a possibility, but this remains his suspicion rather than confirmed fact.

## Confirmed Facts

- The dragging sound from the first night left a shallow drag mark outside the East Shed.
- The drag mark contains broken bamboo leaves and gray-white powder.
- At yin hour, new servant disciples are sent to the servant yard for work assignment.
- Work assignments include sweeping leaves at Qinglu Terrace, carrying water at the Sword-washing Channel, and paving steps.
- Li Cheng and the round-faced boy are assigned to paving steps.
- The paving task uses gray-blue bricks with markings on the back.
- Some bricks are cold to the touch.
- Some ground under the line sounds hollow when tapped.
- Turning one brick changes the white vapor and coldness around it.
- Darker old bricks have heavier weight and red traces in their markings.
- A cracked section exists near the pine tree.
- Li Cheng fills the hollow area before laying a darker old brick.
- The darker old brick briefly lights with red markings when laid.
- The fat steward tells Li Cheng: "Do not sleep too deeply tonight."

## Observations Not Yet Explained

- The cause of the midnight dragging sound remains unknown.
- The meaning of the gray-white powder remains unknown.
- The purpose of the brick markings remains unknown.
- The reason the darker old brick lights briefly remains unknown.
- The relationship between the paving task and the night disturbance is not confirmed.

## Li Cheng's Inferences

- Li Cheng thinks the paving task is not as simple as it appears.
- Li Cheng thinks the darker old bricks may be meant for the cracked section rather than normal paving.
- Li Cheng suspects the previous night's disturbance may not have fully gone away.

These are Li Cheng's thoughts and should not be treated as objective canon until later confirmed.
