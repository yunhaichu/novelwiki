# Chapter 002 State

## Review Result

ALLOW as golden-three chapter 2.

## Golden-Three Function

Chapter 2 serves: Bad Work Becomes an Entrance.

It proves that the three-month pressure is real, that work assignment controls cultivation access, and that unwanted work can become a usable crack if Zhou Ping actively chooses it.

## Chapter Summary

On the first work-assignment morning, Zhou Ping observes that old probationers with token marks receive better or earlier work while new probationers wait to be assigned. Zhao Steward assigns Zhou Ping and Wang Man to the lower spring, but Zhou Ping steps forward and chooses the dirty bucket work near the kitchen instead. Other new probationers laugh at him, but Zhao Steward changes Zhou Ping's assignment to kitchen-back bucket washing while Wang Man and Liu Er are sent to the lower spring. Zhou Ping pays the cost through smell, dirt, broken skin, and being avoided, but he finishes earlier and reaches the qi-guiding lesson before Wang Man and Liu Er. He stands outside the fifth line with a kitchen-back wooden ladle as proof of assignment, hears one complete key rule, and sees Zhao Steward add another dot beside his name.

## Reader Reward Ledger

- Reader expectation: Low-status unwanted work can become leverage.
- Obstacle: New probationers are passively assigned bad work, and bad work can cost access to the qi-guiding lesson.
- Protagonist action: Zhou Ping volunteers for the dirty bucket work because it is closer to the lesson area.
- Visible gain: Zhao Steward visibly changes Zhou Ping's assignment; Zhou Ping reaches the lesson early, stands outside the fifth line, and hears a complete instruction.
- Witness or reaction: Wang Man sees Zhou Ping separate from the lower-spring assignment and later sees him standing ahead; Liu Er mocks Zhou Ping but is sent to the lower spring and later cannot speak; other probationers react to the smell and the wooden ladle; Zhao Steward adds another register dot.
- Next possibility: The register dots and Zhou Ping's willingness to take unwanted work may become a separate path of attention, but not a clean reward.

## Emotional Ledger

- Pressure: New probationers are expected to receive bad assignments and lose access to learning.
- Protagonist action: Zhou Ping actively chooses the dirty work others avoid.
- Cost: He becomes dirty, smells bad, breaks skin, is avoided, and creates distance from Wang Man.
- Visible compensation: He avoids lower-spring work, arrives earlier, stands closer, and hears one complete key rule.
- Who notices: Wang Man, Liu Er, other probationers, the kitchen-back worker, and Zhao Steward.
- New expectation: Zhou Ping can turn rejected work into access, but others may resent or exploit this.
