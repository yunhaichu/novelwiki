# Qingheng Glossary

This file controls terms allowed in Qingheng drafts.

Do not introduce new sect terms, place names, job labels, evaluation labels, or shorthand unless they are added here first.

## Rule

Use plain Chinese whenever possible.

A term is allowed only if:

- it appears in this glossary, or
- it is ordinary everyday language, or
- its meaning is shown immediately in the sentence or action.

If a term is not in this glossary, do not use it as shorthand.

## Allowed Terms

### Qingheng Sect

Meaning: the sect Zhou Ping enters.

Allowed in draft: 青衡宗

### Servant probation

Meaning: a temporary servant-track status before becoming a real sect member.

Allowed in draft: 杂役试用

Do not shorten to: 试用者身份线, 杂役线, 试用轨, 杂役轨

### Servant probation wood token

Meaning: the gray wood token carried by probationers. It has monthly marks on the back.

Allowed in draft:

- 灰木牌
- 木牌
- 木牌背后的刻痕
- 木牌背后刻了一道 / 两道 / 三道

Do not use bare shorthand:

- 有痕
- 一道痕的
- 两道痕的
- 月痕线

### Register

Meaning: Zhao Steward's written record of servant probationers.

Allowed in draft:

- 册子
- 赵管事的册子
- 册上的记号
- 名字后面的一点 / 一笔 / 一道短横

Do not over-define yet:

- hidden evaluation system
- register track
- second path
- register layer

These may be used only in wiki notes, not in character dialogue or narration before the story confirms them.

### Spiritual root

Meaning: cultivation aptitude shown by the testing jade.

Allowed in draft:

- 灵根
- 土灵根
- 三种灵根混在一起
- 灵根很弱
- 灵根太杂

Do not use unexplained evaluation words:

- 薄
- 淡根
- 杂薄
- 劣根

### Family offering

Meaning: money, grain, or resources a family can provide to the sect.

Allowed in draft:

- 家里能供钱粮
- 每年能送多少粮和钱
- 家里没有能供的东西

Avoid unexplained shorthand:

- 供奉
- 家资

These can appear only if the surrounding sentence explains they mean money and grain.

### Outer sect

Meaning: formal beginner disciple track, higher than servant probation.

Allowed in draft:

- 外门
- 外门弟子
- 外门册

Clarifying sentence preferred when first used:

- 进外门，才算正式被青衡宗收下。

### East Shed

Meaning: lodging assigned to Zhou Ping and Wang Man.

Allowed in draft: 东棚

First appearance must show it is a sleeping shed or lodging.

### Kitchen back area

Meaning: the area behind the kitchen, close to the qi-guiding lesson ground.

Allowed in draft:

- 厨房后面
- 厨房后墙外

Do not use shorthand until the place is well established:

- 灶后

### Lower mountain well

Meaning: a water source down the mountain path. Carrying water from there is slow and tiring.

Allowed in draft:

- 山腰水井
- 去山腰水井挑水
- 下山挑水的地方

Do not use unexplained shorthand:

- 下泉

### Qi-guiding lesson

Meaning: the short daily lesson before evening class where probationers hear basic instructions for drawing qi.

Allowed in draft:

- 引气课
- 晚课前的一刻钟引气
- 每天晚课前那一小段教引气的时间

### Qi sense / response

Meaning: early internal response during cultivation practice.

Allowed in draft:

- 气感
- 感到气
- 引气入身
- 小腹发热但不一定是气

Avoid vague progress labels:

- 入门感
- 灵气反馈
- 进度

### Incense timing

Meaning: the incense used to time qi-guiding lessons.

Allowed in draft:

- 香
- 红线
- 晚课用的香
- 绑在香身上的红线

Do not use abstract terms in draft:

- 香线系统
- 计时权
- 课程时长机制

## Banned Pattern

Do not create compact labels that sound like systems unless the story has already taught them.

Bad examples:

- 册上路径
- 木牌线
- 杂役轨
- 烂活入口
- 外门名额
- 三杂灵根，薄
- 有痕就不用等

Use plain sentences instead.

## Review Requirement

Before approving a chapter, scan for:

- unexplained terms
- shorthand labels
- system-like phrases
- place names not shown in scene
- evaluation words not explained by action

If found, revise into plain language or add the term here with a clear first-use rule.
