# 周成

Novel ID: `urban_three_seconds`

Canon status: initial setup.

## Identity

Name: 周成

Age: 27

Role: 青禾便利店夜班员工，兼帮附近快递点临时看件。

## Story Function

Protagonist.

A very ordinary person who sees a few seconds of image from an object and does not know how to explain it.

## Current Status

- works night shift;
- short on money;
- afraid of losing work;
- knows the store, back alley, nearby residents, and late-night routines.

## Default Behavior

Avoids trouble.

If something strange happens, first reaction is to say nothing and check again later.

## Environmental Modulation

Around customers:

- polite, tired, avoids argument.

Around police:

- nervous, answers too carefully, afraid of sounding guilty.

Around desperate family members:

- wants to help, but worries they will ask how he knows.

## Core Protected Object

His job and ordinary life.

He does not have much, so being dragged into trouble is dangerous.

## Threshold Behavior

If he believes someone may really be hurt, he will take small risks to check ordinary evidence first.

He will not immediately shout impossible claims.

## Voice

Plain, short, hesitant when hiding something.

He should not sound like a detective or a chosen person.

## Ability Boundary

He sometimes sees a few seconds of image when touching an object recently used or carried by someone.

No approved ability name.

## Not Yet Confirmed

- why this happens;
- whether it can be controlled;
- whether he can see more than a few seconds later.
