# Base Settings

Novel ID: `urban_three_seconds`

Canon status: initial setup.

## Genre Foundation

都市异能。

现实城市为主，异能只作为小偏差出现。前三章不建立异能体系，不出现组织招募，不出现专有能力名。

## Core Operating Logic

周成碰到别人刚用过的东西时，有时会看见几秒钟画面。

这些画面：

- 很短；
-不一定完整；
-没有声音或声音很模糊；
-只能看到和物品有关的一小段；
-无法主动选择；
-不能保证每次都发生。

## Ability Boundary

At opening stage the ability can:

- show a few seconds of image connected to an object recently used or carried by someone;
- cause dizziness, eye pain, nausea, or brief blankness;
- give a clue that still needs ordinary verification.

It cannot:

- read minds;
- see the future;
- identify everyone automatically;
- show full crime process;
- provide exact address unless visible in the image;
- be explained to strangers without suspicion;
- work as legal proof.

The ability has no approved name in the first three chapters.

## Identity / Authority Rules

周成 is an ordinary citizen and convenience store worker.

He has no police authority, no technical investigation tools, no special contacts, no money, and no official reason to access surveillance.

He can:

- ask coworkers;
- check store receipts if allowed;
- look at public places;
- tell a family member or police what he claims to have seen;
- be suspected if his explanation is strange.

He cannot:

- demand police action;
- access private surveillance freely;
- obtain phone records;
- detain people;
- prove a vision.

## Places

### 青禾便利店

A small neighborhood convenience store on 丰南路.

Functions:

- night-shift work;
- small deliveries and pickup parcels;
- lost-and-found items;
- nearby students and residents buy water, cigarettes, instant noodles, medicine, batteries, umbrellas.

### 民安小区

An older residential compound near the convenience store.

Functions:

- neighbors know faces but not everything;
- old residents talk;
- corridors, back gate, bike shed, and small shops provide ordinary clues.

### 东桥派出所

Local police station.

Function:

- can receive missing-person report;
- will not fully trust unexplained information from 周成;
- acts through ordinary procedure first.

### 南城大学

Local university.

Function:

- source of students, part-time workers, and late-night foot traffic;
- not a conspiracy location at opening.

## Social Behavior Rules

普通人不会因为周成说“我看见了画面”就相信他。

Family members of a missing person may be desperate but still suspicious.

Police may listen but will ask ordinary questions:

- how do you know;
- where did you see it;
- why didn't you report earlier;
- did you touch the phone;
- are you connected to the person.

## Opening Chapter Constraints

Chapter 1 may show:

- 青禾便利店夜班;
- 周成's money/work pressure;
- lost phone;
- first short image after touch;
- ordinary attempt to dismiss it as dizziness.

Chapter 1 must not show:

- named ability;
- full crime answer;
- police trusting him immediately;
- supernatural explanation;
- special organization.

Chapter 2 may show:

- missing person's family looking for the phone;
- 周成 realizing the image may be real;
- ordinary verification attempt;
- suspicion toward him.

Chapter 3 may show:

- 周成 trying to give useful information without saying the impossible part;
- a small clue confirmed;
- police/family pressure;
- ability causing cost.

## Forbidden Setting Moves

- No ability name in first three chapters.
- No system panel.
- No organization recruitment.
- No future sight.
- No instant police trust.
- No perfect clue.
- No coined terms where ordinary description works.
- No summary voice; use xiaobai prose.

## Pending Setting Gaps

- Exact origin of ability unknown.
- Whether ability grows later unknown.
- Full missing-person case unknown.
- Whether 周成 can control it later unknown.
