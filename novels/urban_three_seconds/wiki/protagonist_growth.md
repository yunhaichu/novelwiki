# Protagonist Growth Track

Novel ID: `urban_three_seconds`

Protagonist: 周成

Canon status: initial setup.

## Initial Position

周成，27 岁，青禾便利店夜班员工，兼帮附近快递点临时看件。

He is ordinary, tired, short on money, and afraid of losing work.

## Current Weakness

- no confidence;
- avoids trouble;
- does not know how to explain impossible information;
- fears being treated as suspicious;
- needs the job and cannot freely offend customers, police, or residents.

## Useful Starting Skill

- knows the store, back door, small alley, customers, pickup parcels, and late-night routine;
- remembers small details because night shift is repetitive;
- can talk to neighborhood people without seeming official.

## Starting Misconception

He thinks keeping quiet is always safer.

## Starting Fear

Being blamed for something he tried to help with.

## Large Trend Pressure

In ordinary city life, information has to be explained through ordinary channels.

周成 may see a few seconds of truth, but if he cannot explain how he knows, the truth may become suspicion against him.

## Growth Direction

He should grow from someone who avoids trouble into someone who can help carefully, using ordinary verification, witnesses, objects, and timing.

Not by becoming:

- detective genius;
- police consultant immediately;
- master of a power system;
- fearless hero.

Active growth stage: Stage 1 — Think It Is Dizziness.

## Growth Stages

### Stage 1: Think It Is Dizziness

Capability:

- sees a few seconds from an object once.

Pressure:

- night shift, fatigue, job insecurity.

Lesson:

- the picture may not be imagination.

Cost:

- dizziness;
- confusion;
- possible mistake at work.

Still cannot:

- trigger it reliably;
- explain it;
- know what happened before or after the few seconds.

### Stage 2: Verify With Ordinary Things

Capability:

- can compare the image with real places or objects.

Pressure:

- family/friends/police ask how he knows.

Lesson:

- he needs ordinary evidence before speaking.

Cost:

- looks suspicious;
- may be caught checking things he should not check.

### Stage 3: Help Without Saying Everything

Capability:

- can give a partial clue in ordinary language.

Pressure:

- someone needs help now, but his explanation is weak.

Lesson:

- helping badly can make him look guilty.

Cost:

- police questions;
- store trouble;
- relationship pressure.

## Chapter 1 Contract

Establish:

- ordinary night shift;
- lost phone;
- first few-second image;
- 周成 dismisses it or is unsure.

Do not establish:

- ability name;
- full missing person answer;
- police trust;
- organization;
- power system.
