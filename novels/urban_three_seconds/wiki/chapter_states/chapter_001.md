# Chapter 001 State

Novel ID: `urban_three_seconds`

Chapter title: 手机

Canon status: approved test draft state.

## Chapter Function

Opening chapter. Establishes ordinary night-shift life, lost phone, first few-second image, and one concrete detail that may prove the image was not just dizziness.

## Confirmed Events

- 周成 works night shift at 青禾便利店.
- A young woman buys water and mints, asks whether customers can leave through the back door, then leaves without taking receipt.
- She leaves a white phone at the counter.
- Phone case contains a 南城大学 campus card showing the name 林晓雨.
- 周成 touches the phone and sees a very short image: 林晓雨 walking past the dark patch near the sign, someone in a black cap pulling her bag strap, and a hand with a red bandage.
- He hears no clear sound from the image.
- He later sees an unopened mint on the ground near the dark patch.
- He keeps the phone and mint in the lost-and-found box.

## Protagonist State

Growth stage: Stage 1 — Think It Is Dizziness.

Current capability:

- after touching 林晓雨's phone, 周成 sees a few seconds of image.

Limits:

- cannot control it;
- cannot prove it;
- cannot hear clear sound;
- does not know if it is real;
- cannot identify the person in the black cap.

Cost paid:

- brief blackout / dizziness;
- shaken enough that he mishandles a normal sale briefly.

Usable leverage gained:

- phone owner name: 林晓雨;
- image of dark patch outside store;
- red bandage on one hand;
- mint on ground may connect to the image.

## Character State

### 周成

- avoids trouble;
- initially explains the image as exhaustion;
- still checks the place outside.

### 林晓雨

- came into the store around late night;
- asked about the back door;
- left her phone behind;
- may have been followed or grabbed after leaving.

## Organization / Place State

### 青禾便利店

- has a lost-and-found box under the counter;
- front door faces 丰南路;
- sign casts a dark patch outside;
- back door exists but is not for customer use.

## Key Object / Resource State

- 林晓雨's phone: locked; held in lost-and-found box.
- Campus card: visible in case; confirms name and school.
- Mint: found on ground outside; possible ordinary proof.

## Reader Reward Delivered

- First ability event appears without a name.
- One detail from the image may be physically verified.
- The hook is concrete: whether the girl was really grabbed outside the store.

## Reader Debt

- Is 林晓雨 missing or only gone briefly?
- Who had the hand with red bandage?
- Why did she ask about the back door?
- Will 周成 report what he saw?

## Pressure Clock

- Phone remains at store overnight.
- If 林晓雨's family comes looking, 周成 must explain why he kept it and what he knows.
- If he says too much, he may look suspicious.

## Repetition Risk

- Chapter 2 must not repeat just another touch-and-vision.
- It should bring outside pressure: family, police, or ordinary verification.

## Unresolved Questions

- What happened after the image ended?
- Was the black cap person known to 林晓雨?
- Is the mint enough to prove anything?

## Chapter 002 Constraints

Chapter 2 should use the lost phone and physical mint detail.

It may show:

- family or relative looking for 林晓雨;
- 周成 trying to return the phone;
- someone asking why he knows about the back-door/dark-patch detail;
- first suspicion against 周成.

It must not show:

- ability name;
- full explanation;
- police trust;
- exact culprit identity;
- special organization.

Useful next-chapter attractor:

```text
The phone is still in the store when someone comes looking for 林晓雨.
```
