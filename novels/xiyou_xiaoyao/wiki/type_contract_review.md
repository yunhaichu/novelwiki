# Type Contract Review: 西游小妖

## Decision

PASS.

## Main reason

The contract clearly makes survival counterattack the primary promise. Journey to the West and xianxia elements are support structures, not substitutes for protagonist pressure.

## Checks

- Primary genre clarity: PASS. Mythic transmigration / survival counterattack controls the opening.
- Reader promise: PASS. Survival, status movement, rule understanding, and comic reversal are concrete.
- Opening visibility: PASS. Small demon identity, patrol duty, and coming route danger can be shown immediately.
- Chapter 1-3 expectation: PASS. First three chapters have clear survival and deadline targets.
- Genre blend control: PASS. Comedy and canon references support survival rather than replacing it.
- False promise prevention: PASS. It rejects instant power, pure parody, and passive canon-watching.
- Drift risk: PASS. Lore dump and power fantasy risks are named.

None.
