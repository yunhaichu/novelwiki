# Chapter Review: Chapter 001

## Decision

ALLOW.

## Main reason

Chapter 001 passes the upgraded reader-entry gates. A first-time reader can identify the protagonist, his new little-demon body, his low status, the patrol assignment, the danger of White Bone Ridge West Road, his attempt to avoid the route, and the consequence that he is made lead scout.

## Reader entry check

- Can a stranger reader identify the protagonist? PASS. Lu Qing is introduced immediately through being kicked awake and discovering his demon body.
- Can a stranger reader identify immediate trouble? PASS. He is assigned a patrol token for White Bone Ridge West Road.
- Can a stranger reader identify what is at stake? PASS. He knows small demons on famous Journey to the West routes can die easily, and refusing patrol can bring cave punishment.
- Can a stranger reader identify why to read next? PASS. His attempt to avoid danger backfires and makes him lead scout on the road he fears.
- One-sentence recap: A modern man wakes as a weak little demon, recognizes his patrol route as a deadly Journey to the West location, tries to dodge the assignment, and is ordered to lead the patrol instead.
- Failure if any: none severe.

## Genre promise check

- Declared primary promise: mythic transmigration / survival counterattack.
- Visible promise on page: visible. The protagonist is weak, knows enough canon to fear the route, tries a small survival trick, and pays an immediate cost.
- Mismatch if any: none. Journey to the West lore supports survival pressure rather than replacing it.

Reason:

The final contrast line is polished, but it is grounded in the concrete role reversal and does not replace the chapter's actual consequence. It may be kept for this test, though later chapters should avoid repeated aphoristic closings.

- Why it passes: the chapter already lands on a concrete token, route, and lead-scout consequence before the summary. The line is not the only hook.
