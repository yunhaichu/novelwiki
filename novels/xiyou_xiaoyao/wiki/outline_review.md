# Outline Review: 西游小妖

## Decision

PASS.

## Main reason

The outline serves the type contract and keeps the primary promise on survival counterattack from a low-status demon position. It avoids early lore overload by making Sun Wukong an approaching deadline rather than an immediate on-page opponent.

## Scores

- Type contract alignment: PASS. Survival counterattack remains primary; Journey to the West lore supports danger and rule pressure.
- Reader entry feasibility: PASS. Chapter 1 can show little demon body, roll call, patrol token, route danger, and first survival choice without full lore.
- Hook strength: PASS. A powerless small demon realizing he is on the route of a lethal mythic event is clear.
- Goal clarity: PASS. Survive patrol and avoid the doomed road.
- Relationship tension: PASS. Hei Ya and Mao Qi create immediate cave-level pressure.
- Main genre promise relevance: PASS. Progress is safer assignment, minor token, route timing, low-rank leverage.
- Consequence / escalation capacity: PASS. First route-avoidance choice can create suspicion and future duty changes.
- Expansion capacity: PASS. Low-rank demon survival can scale through route, rules, tokens, and known mythic events.
- Consistency risk: manageable. Canon knowledge must remain incomplete.

## Required fixes if REVISE

None.
