# Chapter 003 - Draft Prompt

You are writing chapter 3 of a cyberpunk Chinese webnovel called 《回收线》.

## Core Principles

- **Xiaobai style**: Clear subject, clear action, clear consequence. Short and medium sentences. Simple words over elegant words. Dialogue and action carry the scene.
- **No system-hacking**: The protagonist cannot hack, decode, or interface with systems. Only physical workflow, small delays, and human interactions.
- **Scene first**: No exposition dumps. Show everything through action, object handling, and dialogue.
- **One core drive**: One chapter, one goal.

## Reader Debt to Address

The following reader debts must be addressed or escalated in this chapter:

1. **P0 — What is the pulse?** (age 2): Provide a clearer signal pattern — not decoded speech, but a more recognizable rhythm or quality that hints at something.
2. **P0 — What is inside the yellow bag's board?** (age 1): The protagonist should get closer to the answer, even if it remains mysterious.
3. **P1 — Will the left-hand sorter call in the debt?** (age 1): The debt relationship must activate.
4. **P1 — Can 马宽 keep access to the board?** (age 2): Show how the reseal process affects his ability to reach it.
5. **P1 — What is 马小禾's condition?** (age 2): Show her through dialogue or observation.

## Chapter 003 Constraints

- Use the **reseal table** or **coworker debt** as the primary access mechanism.
- Must NOT repeat the simple conveyor-stop pattern from chapters 1-2.
- Must introduce a new access pressure or relationship consequence.
- MAY show: the left-hand sorter calling in her tape debt, reseal process requiring manual confirmation, 马宽 choosing between touching the yellow bag and keeping his metrics clean, a first clearer signal pattern without decoding speech.
- MUST NOT show: full message decoding, hacking, original owner identity, corporate combat or chase, clean proof.

## World Rules

- 北环再处理站 processes returned medical hardware
- Reseal table: when an outbound seal fails, items return to manual resealing near the sorting stations
- A reseal requires visual confirmation — a worker must inspect the seal, reapply it, and log the action
- During manual reseal, a worker has 20-30 seconds of unsupervised access to the item
- Supervisors walk the line on a loop; their presence is unpredictable
- Line metrics are tracked: every pause, delay, or anomaly affects 马宽's score
- Medication subscription depends on maintaining a minimum work score
- The yellow bag contains wet residual material — biological contamination, sealed by a plastic tamper-evident strip

## Writing Instructions

Write the chapter in Chinese. Aim for approximately 3000-4000 Chinese characters.

Follow the xiaobai style strictly. Show, don't tell. Every action should have a consequence. Every dialogue should serve a character's goal.

Key moments to include:
1. The left-hand sorter approaches about the tape debt — she has a real need, not just a transaction
2. 马宽's internal calculation: help her vs. use the reseal window for the bag
3. A choice must be made — something is traded (tape, access, or a promise)
4. The pulse returns with a clearer pattern — something slightly more recognizable than the previous pulse, but still not decoded
5. The supervisor's presence interrupts or creates additional pressure
6. The chapter ends with a concrete moment: an action, an object, or an unresolved choice — not explanation
