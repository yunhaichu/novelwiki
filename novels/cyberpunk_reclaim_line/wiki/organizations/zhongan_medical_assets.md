# 中安医疗资产

Novel ID: `cyberpunk_reclaim_line`

Canon status: initial setup.

## Public Legitimacy

中安医疗资产 sells itself as affordable subscription-based medical access.

Public language:

- affordable care;
- patient safety;
- compliant recovery;
- sustainable refurbishing;
- data protection.

## Real Interests

- maintain subscription dependence;
- recover hardware value;
- avoid data-liability scandals;
- keep low-tier patients replaceable;
- push line errors downward onto contractors and workers.

## Operating Boundary

The corporation does not need to be violent first.

It can act through:

- medication subscription delay;
- credit penalty;
- work suspension;
- contamination flag;
- audit hold;
- device recall;
- legal hold.

## Embodied Agents

Initial likely agents:

- line supervisor;
- compliance clerk;
- retrieval courier;
- clinic kiosk operator;
- city-credit liaison.

Do not show executives early unless the plot has earned higher-level attention.

## Action Ladder

1. automated warning;
2. shift metric penalty;
3. supervisor check;
4. sealed tray / audit hold;
5. work suspension;
6. medication subscription delay;
7. compliance interview;
8. city-credit escalation.

## Scene Use

中安 should appear through ordinary procedures before overt threat.

A blocked medicine refill or sealed tray is stronger than a speech about corporate evil.
