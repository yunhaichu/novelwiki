## Naming Rules

- Legal names should be ordinary and socially grounded.
- Corporate systems may add cold labels: work ID, route ID, bay ID, case ID, subscription ID.
- Coworkers may use number nicknames, shortened surnames, work-station names, or defect-based nicknames.
- Do not make every name sound cyberpunk. The world should add the cyberpunk layer through systems, not through all legal names.
- Record rejected names and reasons when a candidate fails.
