# Protagonist Growth Track

Novel ID: `cyberpunk_reclaim_line`

Protagonist: 马宽 / R-219 / 二一九

Canon status: initial setup.

## Initial Position

Starting role: contract worker on 三号回收线 at 北环再处理站.

Starting weakness:

- low credit score;
- medical subscription tied to employment;
- no system admin access;
- no legal leverage;
- old auditory implant causes pain under abnormal signal;
- sister's medication depends on continued work.

Starting useful skill:

- can distinguish physical damage categories on returned implants;
- knows timing gaps on the recycling line;
- understands which tray labels cause delays;
- has patience from repetitive shift work;
- can hear faint offline handshakes through an obsolete analog hearing implant.

Starting misconception:

- he believes line rules are harsh but predictable;
- he does not yet understand that failed erasure can be pushed downward as worker fault.

Starting fear:

- losing medication access for 马小禾.

Starting dependency:

- sister 马小禾's medical subscription requires continuous employment and regular payment of the medical device lease. If he loses his job or is flagged as unreliable, his sister loses medication access. This dependency is the primary reason he cannot simply quit or openly rebel.

What he cannot do at the start:

- hack systems;
- decode residual data;
- identify the original implant owner;
- stop corporate retrieval;
- prove an erasure failure;
- protect himself from audit if flagged.

## Large Trend Pressure

Medical devices are leased through subscriptions.

Corporations profit from repair, repossession, refurbishing, and patient dependency.

Low-level workers are convenient fault sinks when hardware, data, or liability moves wrong.

马宽 cannot change the system. He can only delay, misroute, preserve, or hide a small object long enough to create leverage.

## Growth Direction

By the end of the first major arc, 马宽 should become:

- someone who understands the recycling line as a power system, not just a job;
- someone who can use timing, labels, trays, coworker debts, and physical objects as leverage;
- someone who can preserve selected offline traces without exposing himself too early.

Not by becoming:

- a master hacker;
- a corporate super-agent;
- a revolutionary leader immediately;
- a chosen user of an AI system.

Core growth verb:

```text
hear -> doubt -> preserve -> misroute -> trade -> expose selectively
```

## Growth Stages

### Stage 1: Hear The Wrong Pulse

Current capability:

- can hear faint offline handshake residue from very close range.

Primary pressure:

- shift metrics, scanner timing, medication subscription, supervisor checks.

Lesson to learn:

- hearing something is not proof and may create danger.

Usable leverage gained:

- knowledge that one supposedly erased implant is not dead;
- one possible tray delay or misroute.

Cost paid:

- tinnitus;
- headache;
- risk of line penalty;
- risk to sister's medication if suspended.

Still cannot do:

- decode data;
- prove identity;
- remove implant safely;
- explain anomaly to supervisor.

Forbidden jump:

- no hacking, no full message, no instant ally.

### Stage 2: Preserve Without Owning

Current capability:

- can use ordinary line procedures to delay or misroute an object.

Primary pressure:

- audit checks, sealed trays, coworker suspicion, throughput penalties.

Lesson to learn:

- object control matters more than information possession.

Usable leverage gained:

- the object remains reachable;
- one coworker or supervisor misreads the delay as ordinary line trouble.

Cost paid:

- damaged shift score;
- someone else may be blamed;
- body strain from signal exposure.

Still cannot do:

- read contents;
- contact owner;
- take item home;
- safely trust anyone.

### Stage 3: Trade A Delay

Current capability:

- can trade access, silence, or a shift favor for time near the object.

Primary pressure:

- medication subscription deadline.

Lesson to learn:

- every delay has a price and creates another holder of the secret.

Usable leverage gained:

- temporary access window;
- minor debt relationship;
- clearer category of residual signal.

Cost paid:

- favor owed;
- coworker leverage over him;
- increased audit exposure.

### Stage 4: Selective Exposure

Current capability:

- can show the trace to one actor who can act but cannot be trusted fully.

Primary pressure:

- corporate recovery team or city-credit freeze.

Lesson to learn:

- proof is not protection unless it changes another person's action.

Usable leverage gained:

- a reluctant ally, blackmail route, protected object, or access shift.

Cost paid:

- loss of invisibility;
- higher-level attention;
- personal/family risk.

## Invisible Hand Rule

Allowed:

- place 马宽 near low-value but liability-heavy objects;
- make the line's ordinary timing gaps matter;
- force him to choose between job safety and object preservation;
- let corporate pressure act through routine audits before violence.

Not allowed:

- instant hacking;
- complete data decoding;
- sudden elite ally;
- corporation collapsing from one discovery;
- sister used only as sentimental decoration without concrete medication pressure.

- the fact that hearing is not proof;

Chapter 1 must not establish:

- identity of the original owner;
- hacking success;
