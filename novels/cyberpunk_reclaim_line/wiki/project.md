# Project

Novel ID: `cyberpunk_reclaim_line`

Working title: 《回收线》

Status: new test novel using updated Name Gate and wiki bootstrap flow.

## Type Promise

Cyberpunk future city focused on lower-city labor, medical debt, corporate credit, recycled body hardware, and data residue.

The story starts from a recycling line rather than from elite hackers, mercenaries, or corporate executives.

## Core Reader Promise

- The future city is expensive, monitored, medically rented, and materially recycled.
- The protagonist begins as a contract worker inside the lowest useful layer of corporate infrastructure.
- The main opportunity comes from discarded body hardware and incomplete data erasure.
- The protagonist cannot defeat corporate systems by hacking terminals. He can only preserve, delay, misroute, hide, or reinterpret small things in physical workflows.
- Every advantage costs body strain, job risk, medical subscription risk, exposure, or debt.

## Protagonist

Legal name: 马宽

Work ID: R-219

Coworker nickname: 二一九

Starting role: contract worker on 三号回收线 at 北环再处理站.

Core anomaly: an obsolete analog hearing implant, left from a cheap childhood repair, can sometimes hear low-frequency offline handshakes from supposedly erased implants.

## Core Contradiction

中安医疗资产 treats returned implants as material inventory and data liability.

马宽 sometimes hears that a supposedly dead device still carries a trace linked to a living or recently disconnected person.

If he reports it, the device is sealed and he may lose his job for touching contaminated data.

If he hides it, he risks credit penalties, prosecution, or termination of his sister's medication subscription.

## Story Engine

```text
medical subscription debt
-> contract labor on implant recycling line
-> returned hardware with incomplete erasure
-> protagonist hears an offline handshake
-> he preserves or misroutes one object
-> a person, route, or debt record changes
-> corporate and bodily pressure rise
```

## What This Story Is Not

- Not superhero hacking.
- Not instant AI awakening.
- Not a terminal-command victory over corporations.
- Not neon decoration without economic function.
- Not system-interface fiction.
- Not a detective story carried by reports instead of physical objects and people.

## Genre Mode

Genre mode: cyberpunk
Subgenre: lower-city labor / cyberpunk realism
Primary pressure carrier: medical subscription debt, corporate credit, job security, bodily exposure
What scenes make readers continue: physical workflows, small interventions in process, tense moments where a wrong move costs the protagonist real resources
What scenes feel off-genre: terminal-command hacking victories, system-panel power fantasies, neon decoration without material cost, detective exposition
Key forbidden moves: protagonist defeats corporations by hacking, instant AI awakening, superhero capabilities


## First Arc Direction

马宽 must keep his sister's medication subscription active while handling a returned neural implant that should have been erased but still pulses offline.

The first arc should force him to choose between:

- doing the job correctly and staying invisible;
- preserving one illegal trace and becoming useful to someone he cannot trust.

## Initial Reader Hook

A recycling worker hears a pulse from a dead implant that should not be able to speak.

He cannot prove it without touching the thing again.

Touching it may cost his family medication access.
