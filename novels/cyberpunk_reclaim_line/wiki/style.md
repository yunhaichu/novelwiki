# Style

Novel ID: `cyberpunk_reclaim_line`

Canon status: initial setup.

## Prose Direction

Plain, concrete, low-level cyberpunk.

Avoid ornamental neon descriptions unless they directly affect access, cost, surveillance, work, or bodily risk.

The prose should feel like the city is expensive and worn down, not merely stylish.

## Texture

Preferred details:

- scratched scanner glass;
- work gloves with patched fingertips;
- conveyor belt pauses;
- medication kiosk lights;
- blocked cards;
- worker ID tags;
- damp cables;
- disinfectant smell;
- plastic seals;
- cheap implants buzzing in old bone;
- transport gate refusing entry.

## Language Rules

Use concise Chinese narrative.

Do not over-explain corporate systems. Show them through denied access, delayed medicine, line penalties, supervisors, seals, trays, and object movement.

Avoid cyberpunk clichés:

- endless neon rain;
- cool hacker monologues;
- black trench-coat mystique;
- AI god voice;
- corporation explained by essay;
- terminal screens as climax.

## Dialogue Rules

Characters speak from job, cost, time, and risk.

- Workers speak in short practical lines.
- Supervisors speak in throughput, error, seal, audit, docked credit, and contamination terms.
- Corporate agents use compliance language, not naked cruelty.
- 马宽 avoids explanation unless it protects his sister, job, or immediate access.

## Ability Presentation

The old auditory implant produces sound and bodily cost.

Use:

- hum;
- pulse;
- low click;
- tooth vibration;
- skull pressure;
- nausea;
- imbalance.

Do not use:

- visible energy;
- text popups;
- decoded full messages;
- mystical voice;
- instant hacking.

## Narrative Economy Notes

If a sentence explains what a blocked gate, docked credit, sealed tray, or unpaid medication already shows, delete it.

Prefer one concrete blocked action over a paragraph of social explanation.
