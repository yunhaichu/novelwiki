# 北环再处理站

Novel ID: `cyberpunk_reclaim_line`

Canon status: initial setup.

## Identity

A lower-city facility that processes returned medical implants and body hardware for 中安医疗资产.

## Function In World

Not a secret lab.

It is a legal, low-status facility where corporate systems recover value and push liability downward.

## Main Process

Returned hardware flow:

```text
intake crate
-> visual damage check
-> ID scan
-> erasure verification
-> physical sorting
-> refurbish / strip / melt / quarantine / legal hold
```

## 三号回收线

马宽 works on 三号回收线.

The line handles small implants, hearing units, limb-interface boards, sleep regulators, pain filters, and low-tier neural ports.

## Sorting Categories

- clean shell: casing can be stripped or melted;
- refurb core: sent to repair contractor;
- wet return: biological contamination, sealed tray;
- dead board: electronics scrap;
- locked memory: failed erasure, legal hold;
- misread object: scanner cannot classify, supervisor must clear.

## Why Sorting Matters

Sorting is economic and legal.

- clean shells recover metals;
- refurb cores become low-tier subscription parts;
- dead boards are shredded for trace metals;
- wet returns cost money and slow the line;
- locked memory creates liability;
- misread objects hurt shift metrics and may trigger audit.

## Low-Level Worker Power

Workers cannot certify clean data.

Workers can:

- delay a tray by claiming jam, crack, wet return, or misread;
- move items within assigned range;
- preserve a physical object briefly;
- trade silence or tool access with coworkers.

## Scene Use

The station should appear through trays, seals, scanners, gloves, line pauses, docking penalties, supervisor calls, and blocked physical routes.
