# Project

Novel ID: `cyberpunk_offline_city`

Working title: 《离线城》

Status: test novel using the updated minimal-flow process. Name Gate has not passed yet.

## Type Promise

Cyberpunk future city.

Low-level workers, corporate infrastructure, debt-bound bodies, recycled implants, data residue, and survival under corporate scoring systems.

## Core Reader Promise

- A future city where identity, health, work, transport, and speech are mediated by corporate systems.
- The protagonist starts at the bottom of the implant-recycling chain.
- The key opportunity comes from discarded hardware and incomplete data erasure, not from omnipotent hacking.
- Every gain costs access, body strain, debt, or exposure risk.
- The city does not revolve around the protagonist; the protagonist survives through small cracks in large systems.

## Protagonist

Name: `TBD — must pass Name Gate before use.`

Starting role: contract worker at a lower-city implant recycling station.

Core anomaly: an old analog auditory implant can hear low-frequency offline handshakes from some devices after failed erasure.

## Core Contradiction

The city treats discarded implants as material and data waste.

The protagonist can sometimes detect that a supposedly erased device is still carrying a living person's trace.

## Story Engine

```text
corporate scoring and medical debt
-> low-level recycling labor
-> discarded implants with incomplete erasure
-> protagonist detects a residual offline pulse
-> small intervention preserves a device, person, or route
-> pressure from corporation, debt, and exposure rises
```

## What This Story Is Not

- Not superhero hacking.
- Not instant AI awakening.
- Not a world where one terminal command defeats corporations.
- Not pure detective exposition.
- Not system-interface fiction.
- Not neon decoration without material cost.

## First Arc Direction

The protagonist must decide whether to protect one illegally retained data trace from the recycling chain, while staying employed long enough to keep a family member's medication subscription active.
