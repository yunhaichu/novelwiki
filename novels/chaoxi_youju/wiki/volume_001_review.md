# Volume 001 Review

## Decision

PASS.

## Main Reason

The volume has a clear local goal: prove the first tide letter and reduce the South Levee bus disaster. It also moves the larger mystery by connecting the incident to Lin Qiming's old case through a missing data segment.

## Checks

### Whole-Story Alignment

PASS.

The volume serves the project outline and does not explain the whole tide-letter system too early.

### Start / End Change

PASS.

Start: Lin Zhao has no credibility and no proof.

End: the first letter is publicly supported by an altered incident, but Lin Zhao pays a memory cost and the father case gains a new data gap.

### Main Genre Promise

PASS.

- Suspense: predicted disaster is tested.
- Light science fiction: one rule and one cost are established.
- Urban pressure: platform takeover threatens old public data.

### Reader Reward

PASS.

The reader gets a visible test of the first letter and proof that the mechanism is real, while a larger cost opens.

### Relationship Change

PASS.

Lin Zhao and Song Zhiyao move from suspicion toward limited cooperation. Xu He moves from platform receiver to active threat.

### Midpoint Turn

PASS.

Formal warning backfires and brings Song Zhiyao directly onto Lin Zhao.

### Climax Consequence

PASS.

The bus incident is reduced but not erased; Lin Zhao loses a specific memory; old case data gap emerges.

### Drift Prevention

PASS.

The plan names key drifts and has controls.

## Required Follow-Up

Create the 1-10 chapter beat table.

Beat table must ensure:

- No chapter introduces a separate new tide letter as default engine.
- The first letter remains the central object through chapter 10.
- Song Zhiyao acts from her own job pressure, not only as data exposition.
- Qin Man has self-interested pressure around the platform transfer.
- Xu He appears through concrete platform actions, not villain monologue.
