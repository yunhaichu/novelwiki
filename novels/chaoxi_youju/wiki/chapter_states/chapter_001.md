## Chapter Summary

During the final night shift before the old postal center is transferred to a commercial logistics platform, Lin Zhao processes returned mail under an active audit camera. Qin Man warns him not to touch sealed dead-letter trays because platform systems can flag abnormal operations. Lin Zhao notices that tray 7 has shifted even though its seal remains intact. Inside is one letter addressed to him, with a postmark dated tomorrow: 2028-07-13. The note inside predicts a South Levee bus incident at 17:40 with thirty-seven people falling into water. Lin Zhao hides the letter from Qin Man. After Lin Zhao returns the tray, the old sorting machine produces a small slip with a new postmark dated tomorrow and the line: South Levee Station, before 17:40.
