# Story Bible Review

## Decision

PASS.

## Main Reason

The story bible separates planning canon from future reveals, tracks the central objects, defines limited rules, and keeps the tide-letter mechanism partially unknown.

## Checks

### Canon Scope

PASS.

Planning canon and planned future reveals are labeled. Unknowns are not treated as facts.

### Character State

PASS.

Main recurring characters include goal, hidden pressure, flaw, and knowledge state.

### Timeline State

PASS for pre-draft stage.

The bible includes the closure shift and South Levee incident anchors. More scene-level entries must be added after chapters are drafted.

### Rule State

PASS.

Rules include limits and costs. Full mechanism is not overexplained.

### Foreshadow State

PASS.

Planned foreshadows include surface meaning, real meaning, target payoff, and status.

### Object / Resource State

PASS.

Core objects are tracked: first letter, old sorter, route card, data gap.

### Open Questions

PASS.

Open questions have scope and target answer windows.

## Required Follow-Up

After drafting each chapter, update:

- confirmed facts
- timeline anchors
- object holders/status
- open questions
- foreshadow status
- character knowledge state

## Risk Note

The term `潮汐信` should not appear too early in prose unless the story has already shown why Lin Zhao names it that. Use plain wording first: future-dated letter, dead letter, letter addressed to himself.
