# Glossary

This file controls approved terms for `潮汐邮局`.

Use plain Chinese whenever possible. Do not invent compact labels unless they are approved here.

## Approved Terms

### 潮汐信

Meaning: A future-dated letter that appears in the old postal system and predicts a specific event. Its full mechanism is unknown in Volume 1.

Allowed first use:

```text
那封信的邮戳日期写着明天，信里预告了南堤公交事故。林照后来把这类信暂时称为“潮汐信”。
```

Do not use before the story teaches it.

Forbidden shorthand:

```text
潮信
未来信路
潮汐邮路
```

### 旧分拣机

Meaning: The old postal sorting machine in the closing postal center. It can read a second stamp layer not visible to the platform scanner.

Allowed wording:

```text
旧分拣机
旧邮政分拣机
```

Forbidden shorthand:

```text
老机子
潮汐机
读潮机
```

### 第二层邮戳

Meaning: A hidden stamp layer visible only through the old sorting machine. It relates to tide-time marks, but its full rule is unknown.

Allowed wording:

```text
第二层邮戳
旧分拣机读出的另一层邮戳
```

Forbidden shorthand:

```text
潮戳
二层戳
```

### 旧邮路

Meaning: The old postal route records that sometimes overlap with flood, transit, or low-lying-city data.

Allowed wording:

```text
旧邮路
旧邮路记录
旧路线卡
```

Forbidden shorthand:

```text
潮线
旧路子
```

### 平台

Meaning: The commercial logistics platform taking over old postal data and sorting operations.

Allowed wording:

```text
平台
商业物流平台
新平台
```

First use must show it is taking over old postal data.

### 南堤

Meaning: A low-lying urban levee district and bus route area involved in the first predicted incident.

Allowed wording:

```text
南堤
南堤公交
南堤站
```

Do not overload the first chapter with full geography.

### 记忆代价

Meaning: A specific memory loss connected to changing or using a tide letter. It is not fully known until later.

Allowed wording after it is shown:

```text
失去一段具体记忆
记忆代价
```

Do not use as abstract explanation before the reader sees it.

## Low-Anchor Warning

Avoid:

```text
潮起潮落的信
明日之邮
旧城记忆
被潮水寄来
```

These sound poetic but can become empty if not tied to event and cost.

## Review Rule

If a new term appears in draft prose, it must either:

- be added here first
- be ordinary language
- be explained immediately through action or consequence

Otherwise mark REVISE.
