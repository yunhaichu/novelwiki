# Volume 001 Plan

## Volume Number

001

## Volume Title

明日死信

## Volume Role In Whole Story

Volume 1 proves that tide letters are real, introduces the memory cost, binds Lin Zhao and Song Zhiyao into a reluctant partnership, and connects the first disaster warning to Lin Qiming's old case.

## Volume Core Conflict

Lin Zhao receives a future-dated letter predicting a bus disaster. He must verify and act on it before the deadline, but public systems treat him as unreliable, and every step toward proof risks exposing his father's old case.

## Volume External Goal

Prevent or reduce the predicted South Levee bus disaster.

## Volume Inner Pressure

Lin Zhao wants to prove that old postal work and his father were not useless, but he also fears inheriting his father's disgrace and becoming another person deleted by institutional convenience.

## Volume Main Opposition

- Commercial logistics platform taking over old postal data.
- Song Zhiyao's initial suspicion and data-first worldview.
- Police and transit systems that cannot act on unverifiable future warnings.
- Lin Zhao's own fear of becoming a fraud like his father was accused of being.

## Volume Failure Cost

- People die or are badly injured in the bus incident.
- Lin Zhao is treated as a rumor-maker or suspect.
- Old postal data is transferred before he understands the tide letters.
- The father case remains sealed.

## Volume Start State

Lin Zhao is an ordinary old postal sorting worker in a closing facility. He has no credibility, no platform access, and no confirmed proof that the letter is real.

## Volume End State

The bus disaster is changed into a smaller but public incident. Lin Zhao loses a specific memory connected to his father. Song Zhiyao finds the first missing water-level data segment in the old case. Xu He begins moving to seize the old sorting machine and archived postal data.

## Main Genre Promise To Pay In This Volume

- Suspense: prove or disprove the predicted disaster.
- Light science fiction: establish one rule of tide letters and one cost.
- Urban pressure: show old public infrastructure being swallowed by platform logistics.

## Reader Expectation

The reader wants the first letter to matter quickly and wants the protagonist to act under a deadline before he has institutional credibility.

## Primary Reward Pattern

Prediction becomes testable evidence, but each proof creates a new cost.

## Relationship Changes

### Lin Zhao / Song Zhiyao

Surface relation at start: suspected fabricator and data investigator.

Hidden tension: he trusts old routes and lived memory; she trusts live data and institutional records.

Required change by end: she does not fully trust him, but she can no longer dismiss him; she chooses to preserve a data gap instead of reporting him immediately.

### Lin Zhao / Qin Man

Surface relation at start: coworkers in a closing postal center.

Hidden tension: Qin Man wants to survive the transition to the platform; Lin Zhao's choices threaten that.

Required change by end: Qin Man leaks or nearly leaks information, then realizes the platform may erase them both.

### Lin Zhao / Xu He

Surface relation at start: platform receiver and old-system employee.

Hidden tension: Xu He sees old postal data as risk capital; Lin Zhao sees it as public memory.

Required change by end: Xu He identifies Lin Zhao as an obstacle.

## Three-Layer Suspense In This Volume

### Immediate

Will the South Levee bus incident happen at 17:40, and can Lin Zhao change it?

### Midterm

Why does the future-dated letter connect to water levels and the old postal route?

### Longterm / Global Link

Did Lin Qiming hide earlier tide letters inside old postal archives, and what happens if the platform gets them?

## Volume Turning Points

### Opening Imbalance

Lin Zhao receives a dead letter addressed to himself, postmarked tomorrow, warning of the South Levee bus incident.

### First Method / Tool / Resource

The old sorting machine reads a second stamp layer: a tide-time mark invisible to the platform interface.

### Midpoint Turn

Lin Zhao's attempt to warn through formal channels backfires, and Song Zhiyao traces the warning back to him.

### Late Reversal

The bus incident happens differently than written. Lin Zhao's intervention works only partially, proving the letter is real while also proving that changing it changes the cost.

### Volume Climax

Lin Zhao saves several passengers, but afterward he cannot remember a specific day with his father. Song Zhiyao finds that same date missing from old water-level records.

## Required Chapter Groups

### Chapters 1-3

Establish the letter, deadline, old postal center, platform transfer, and first verification method.

### Chapters 4-6

Institutional warning fails, Song Zhiyao investigates Lin Zhao, and the old sorting machine proves the tide-time layer.

### Chapters 7-10

The South Levee incident occurs, the result changes, memory cost appears, father case data gap emerges, and Xu He moves to take control.

## Continuity Objects To Track

- first tide letter
- tomorrow postmark
- old sorting machine
- platform transfer notice
- South Levee bus route
- water-level data gap
- Lin Zhao's memory loss
- Lin Qiming's old case file

## Forbidden Drift In This Volume

- Do not explain the full tide-letter mechanism.
- Do not turn each chapter into a new predicted disaster.
- Do not write postal workplace routine without mystery progress.
- Do not make Song Zhiyao a pure exposition character.
- Do not let memory loss become vague; it must remove a specific memory.
- Do not solve father case in Volume 1.
