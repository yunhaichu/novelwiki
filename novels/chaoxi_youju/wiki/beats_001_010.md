# Beats 001-010

## Beat Table

### Chapter 001

Chapter number: 001

Beat role: Opening hook and first warning.

Main engine: Future-dated dead letter arrives during closure shift.

Opening pressure: Old postal center is being shut down; Lin Zhao is sorting returned mail for transfer to the platform.

Protagonist goal: Finish the transfer without drawing attention and keep one last night of control over the old sorting floor.

Protagonist action: Opens a dead-letter tray that should already be sealed and finds a letter addressed to himself with tomorrow's postmark.

Opposition / obstacle: The platform audit camera is active, and opening sealed dead-letter trays can be treated as a violation.

State change: Lin Zhao changes from closing-shift worker to named recipient of a future warning.

Relationship change: Qin Man sees enough to know Lin Zhao is hiding something but not enough to know what.

Information change: The letter predicts the South Levee bus incident at 17:40 tomorrow.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao has possession of the first tide letter but risks being flagged by the audit system.

Reader reward: Immediate hook, deadline, and personal stake.

Main-system progress: First anomaly appears and becomes testable.

Cost or consequence: Lin Zhao must decide whether to report the letter, hide it, or verify it himself.

Chapter hook: The old sorting machine stamps the sealed letter a second time after he touches it.

What must not happen: Do not explain tide letters. Do not introduce a second predicted disaster.

### Chapter 002

Chapter number: 002

Beat role: First verification attempt.

Main engine: Lin Zhao tests whether the letter is a prank or a machine error.

Opening pressure: The transfer team arrives early to image the old mail database.

Protagonist goal: Determine whether the postmark can be forged.

Protagonist action: Compares the letter against old machine logs and archived misprint records.

Opposition / obstacle: Platform technicians start disconnecting the old sorter before he finishes.

State change: The postmark cannot be matched to any known machine error.

Relationship change: Qin Man urges Lin Zhao to let it go because they need platform jobs.

Information change: The old sorter shows a second time layer that the platform scanner reads as blank.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao gains a method: old machine readable, new platform unreadable.

Reader reward: The anomaly becomes physical evidence, not only a spooky letter.

Main-system progress: First rule hint: old postal equipment can read tide-time marks.

Cost or consequence: Lin Zhao copies logs without authorization.

Chapter hook: The copied log contains Lin Qiming's old employee ID.

What must not happen: Do not make Qin Man only comic relief. Do not explain the father's case through monologue.

### Chapter 003

Chapter number: 003

Beat role: Father case enters the present.

Main engine: The letter's machine log links to Lin Zhao's father.

Opening pressure: Lin Zhao has until the next evening but now risks reopening a disgraced case.

Protagonist goal: Find out why Lin Qiming's ID appears in a new log years after his death.

Protagonist action: Searches archived employee access records and sealed route notes.

Opposition / obstacle: The archive is marked for platform transfer, and some route files are already missing.

State change: The father case becomes connected to the present anomaly.

Relationship change: Qin Man catches Lin Zhao searching restricted files and asks whether he wants both of them fired.

Information change: Lin Qiming once flagged South Levee as an abnormal route during a flood year.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao recovers a route card with South Levee handwritten on it.

Reader reward: Personal mystery connects to disaster warning.

Main-system progress: Old route data may predict or preserve disaster patterns.

Cost or consequence: Lin Zhao misses the chance to report normally before the system locks files.

Chapter hook: A city warning dashboard shows no flood risk for South Levee, contradicting the old route card.

What must not happen: Do not make father case fully clear.

### Chapter 004

Chapter number: 004

Beat role: Formal warning fails.

Main engine: Lin Zhao tries to warn through official channels without revealing the letter.

Opening pressure: The predicted time is less than a day away.

Protagonist goal: Get someone to inspect or delay the South Levee bus route.

Protagonist action: Sends an anonymous warning using old postal emergency routing language.

Opposition / obstacle: The message is treated as a hoax because live water-level data is normal.

State change: Lin Zhao becomes traceable.

Relationship change: Song Zhiyao, a city water-data analyst, is assigned to identify the source of the false warning.

Information change: Current data and old route data disagree.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao loses anonymity.

Reader reward: The warning has consequence; authority responds, but not as Lin Zhao wants.

Main-system progress: Data conflict becomes central.

Cost or consequence: Song Zhiyao starts looking for the sender.

Chapter hook: Song Zhiyao's trace leads to the old postal center, not the public warning terminal Lin Zhao used.

What must not happen: Do not make Song Zhiyao a helper yet.

### Chapter 005

Chapter number: 005

Beat role: Investigator pressure and first direct confrontation.

Main engine: Song Zhiyao confronts Lin Zhao before the predicted time.

Opening pressure: The platform audit and city data trace both point toward Lin Zhao.

Protagonist goal: Avoid being detained or dismissed long enough to act on the warning.

Protagonist action: Shows Song Zhiyao only part of the evidence: the unreadable second stamp, not the full letter.

Opposition / obstacle: Song Zhiyao suspects edited evidence and wants the original letter.

State change: Lin Zhao is no longer investigating alone.

Relationship change: Song Zhiyao moves from remote tracer to direct pressure figure.

Information change: Song Zhiyao reveals South Levee data had a one-minute dropout last week.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao gains a data clue but risks losing the letter.

Reader reward: Suspicion becomes interpersonal conflict; data gap appears.

Main-system progress: Tide-letter warning intersects with city sensor gaps.

Cost or consequence: Song Zhiyao demands to accompany him to South Levee.

Chapter hook: Qin Man sends a message: platform staff are removing the old sorter early.

What must not happen: Do not make Song Zhiyao believe too quickly.

### Chapter 006

Chapter number: 006

Beat role: Choice between evidence and action.

Main engine: Old sorter removal and South Levee deadline collide.

Opening pressure: Lin Zhao can either save the old sorter evidence or go to South Levee.

Protagonist goal: Preserve proof while still acting before 17:40.

Protagonist action: Sends Qin Man instructions to stall platform removal while he goes with Song Zhiyao.

Opposition / obstacle: Qin Man wants a platform job and hesitates to risk himself.

State change: Lin Zhao must depend on someone whose loyalty is uncertain.

Relationship change: Qin Man shifts from passive coworker to active variable.

Information change: The first tide letter may not be enough without old sorter confirmation.

Resource / status change: Evidence and action split into two locations.

Reader reward: Real choice and cost; protagonist cannot cover both fronts.

Main-system progress: Confirms the old sorter is key infrastructure, not just atmosphere.

Cost or consequence: Qin Man stalls badly and attracts Xu He's attention.

Chapter hook: Xu He asks Qin Man who told him the old sorter mattered.

What must not happen: Do not resolve Qin Man's loyalty cleanly.

### Chapter 007

Chapter number: 007

Beat role: Disaster edge begins.

Main engine: Lin Zhao and Song Zhiyao reach South Levee before the predicted time.

Opening pressure: Live data still shows normal conditions, and the bus route is active.

Protagonist goal: Identify what part of the prediction can be changed.

Protagonist action: Uses the route card and letter wording to locate the exact bus stop and bend near the levee.

Opposition / obstacle: Song Zhiyao sees no official reason to stop traffic, and police will not block a route on guesswork.

State change: The disaster location becomes specific.

Relationship change: Song Zhiyao begins testing Lin Zhao's evidence instead of only opposing him.

Information change: The predicted bus number corresponds not to a bus route, but to an old postal route code painted under the stop sign.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao gains a localized proof point.

Reader reward: The clue becomes more precise and surprising without adding a new letter.

Main-system progress: Old postal route and modern transit route overlap.

Cost or consequence: They still cannot stop the bus by authority.

Chapter hook: A bus with the matching route code arrives three minutes early.

What must not happen: Do not make official systems stupid; they need insufficient evidence, not incompetence.

### Chapter 008

Chapter number: 008

Beat role: Intervention and partial change.

Main engine: Lin Zhao acts without official permission.

Opening pressure: The bus is approaching the South Levee bend before 17:40.

Protagonist goal: Prevent the predicted fall into the river.

Protagonist action: Creates a small obstruction using an old postal vehicle access barrier and forces the bus to slow.

Opposition / obstacle: The driver, passengers, and nearby security treat him as dangerous.

State change: The written prediction no longer unfolds exactly.

Relationship change: Song Zhiyao chooses to help by calling in a water-sensor anomaly instead of reporting Lin Zhao first.

Information change: A sudden pavement collapse begins at 17:40, but the slowed bus avoids the deepest section.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao publicly becomes part of the incident.

Reader reward: The warning is real, and intervention matters.

Main-system progress: Tide letters can be changed, but not erased.

Cost or consequence: Lin Zhao is detained at the scene.

Chapter hook: While being questioned, Lin Zhao cannot remember the day his father taught him how to read route cards.

What must not happen: Do not make it a clean heroic save.

### Chapter 009

Chapter number: 009

Beat role: Memory cost and data gap.

Main engine: Aftermath turns proof into personal loss.

Opening pressure: The incident is now public, but Lin Zhao's role is suspicious.

Protagonist goal: Prove he acted from a real warning, not sabotage.

Protagonist action: Tries to describe how he matched the letter to the route card and realizes a memory is missing.

Opposition / obstacle: His missing memory weakens his own explanation.

State change: Memory cost becomes concrete.

Relationship change: Song Zhiyao sees the same date missing from old water-level records and chooses not to file the full accusation yet.

Information change: The lost memory date matches a gap in Lin Qiming's old case file.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao loses internal evidence but gains an external data clue through Song Zhiyao.

Reader reward: Cost lands specifically; father case advances.

Main-system progress: Using/changing a tide letter trades memory for altered outcome.

Cost or consequence: Lin Zhao becomes dependent on records he may not remember how to interpret.

Chapter hook: Xu He files an emergency request to seize the old sorting machine as evidence.

What must not happen: Do not make memory loss vague or decorative.

### Chapter 010

Chapter number: 010

Beat role: Volume close and next pressure.

Main engine: Platform seizure threatens the only machine that can read tide-time marks.

Opening pressure: Xu He uses the public incident to justify taking the old sorter.

Protagonist goal: Stop the old sorter from being removed or preserve its proof layer.

Protagonist action: Uses the missing data date and first letter to force Song Zhiyao into a choice: report him fully, or help copy the unreadable stamp layer first.

Relationship change: Song Zhiyao becomes a reluctant co-conspirator, not a believer.

Information change: The old sorter contains a list of unread tide-time marks, including one tied to Lin Qiming's final route.

Resource / status change: Lin Zhao and Song Zhiyao preserve a partial copy but lose access to the machine.

Reader reward: First volume closes with proof and loss.

Main-system progress: Tide letters are not isolated; they are part of an archived sequence.

Cost or consequence: Xu He now knows Lin Zhao copied something.

Chapter hook: The copied list includes one tide-time mark dated the night Lin Qiming disappeared.

What must not happen: Do not solve the father's case yet.

## Risk Note

The strongest risk is repeating letter-based alerts. Chapters 1-10 must keep the first letter as the central object and let its meaning deepen rather than replace it with new predictions.
