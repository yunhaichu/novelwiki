# 陆沉

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Canon status: updated through Chapter 003.

## Identity

Name: 陆沉

Age: 14

Origin: 白石镇

Starting role: medicine apprentice / low-status candidate / 青岚宗试役.

## Starting Position

陆沉 is not an outer disciple.

He enters 青岚宗 through the 试役 channel after the testing stone shows a weak gray response that does not count as a recognized five-element spirit root.

## Core Desire

Find a real path into cultivation before he is expelled from the trial-worker system.

## Core Fear

Returning to 白石镇 with no path and spending his life as a mortal medicine-hand.

## Starting Useful Skills

- recognizes common herbs and crude medicine residues;
- has patience from low-level physical work;
- can endure hunger, travel fatigue, and social neglect;
- has weak qi-sense from a village fragment;
- can preserve a tiny gray response after contact with the testing stone.

## Default Behavior

Quiet, observant, survival-oriented.

He does not argue when argument has no survival value.

He notices food, workload, authority, material traces, and who has the power to make his life harder.

## Environmental Modulation

Under high-status pressure:

- speaks briefly;
- asks only practical questions;
- does not plead unless pleading has a clear chance.

Around peers:

- does not trust quickly;
- may help only when it also improves survival, work-sharing, debt, or safety;
- avoids showing specialness.

When facing a narrow cultivation chance:

- becomes unusually stubborn;
- accepts humiliation if the chance is real;
- prioritizes preserving the chance over face.

## Threshold Behavior

If the only path to cultivation is about to disappear, 陆沉 will take a physically costly gamble.

He still will not make loud declarations or challenge hierarchy without leverage.

## Specialness Boundary

Current confirmed ability through Chapter 003:

- can preserve a faint gray response;
- can faintly sense that some furnace waste is not fully dead;
- can sometimes use gray response as a costly judgment aid when ordinary senses are insufficient.

Current limits:

- cannot absorb qi;
- cannot trigger visible power;
- cannot identify treasures;
- cannot heal;
- cannot fight with qi;
- cannot leave measurable qi trace;
- cannot prove his value publicly;
- cannot use gray response continuously without fatigue / chest tightness.

## Voice

Plain, economical, grounded in immediate survival.

He asks practical questions:

- whether the method is complete;
- whether a place has access to residue;
- whether food is enough;
- what the rule actually costs.

Avoid making him say grand destiny lines.
