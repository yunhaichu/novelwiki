# 韩管事

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Canon status: approved test setup.

## Identity

Name: 韩管事

Role: back-mountain servant compound manager / low-level sect manager.

Controls early trial-worker assignment, basic ration pressure, and day-to-day discipline.

## Function In Story

Embodies the sect's cost logic at the low level.

He is not a cartoon villain. He is a manager who has seen many trial workers fail and treats them as low-value, replaceable labor.

## Public Role

Maintains order among servants and trial workers.

Assigns tasks, tracks attendance, distributes basic instructions, and prevents waste.

## Real Interest

- keep the compound working;
- avoid deaths or accidents that create trouble;
- avoid wasting resources on hopeless trial workers;
- identify candidates who can be used efficiently;
- keep superiors from noticing failures.

## Default Behavior

Dry, practical, unsentimental.

He explains rules only enough to avoid later disputes.

He does not comfort trial workers.

He does not waste cruelty when ordinary pressure is enough.

## Environmental Modulation

When managing new trial workers:

- states rules bluntly;
- emphasizes cost and dismissal;
- watches for usable skills.

When someone has a practical skill:

- may assign them to a more relevant labor site, not out of kindness, but because it is efficient.

When trouble happens:

- chooses the cheapest fix first;
- avoids escalation unless contamination, injury, theft, or public trouble appears.

## Voice

Short, administrative, practical, slightly contemptuous without dramatics.

He should sound like someone who measures people by work value.
