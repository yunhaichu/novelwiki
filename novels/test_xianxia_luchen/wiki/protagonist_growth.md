# Protagonist Growth Track

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Protagonist: 陆沉

Canon status: approved test setup.

## Initial Position

Starting role: 白石镇药师学徒; 青岚宗试役.

Starting weakness:

- no recognized five-element spirit root;
- no complete cultivation method;
- no sect status;
- no allies;
- mortal body;
- limited food, sleep, and recovery;
- only a fragmentary village breathing method.

Starting useful skill:

- recognizes common herbs;
- knows basic drying, sorting, and residue handling;
- has survival patience from travel and low-level work;
- can preserve a faint gray response after testing.

Starting misconception:

- he believes complete orthodox method may be enough if he works hard;
- he does not yet know that resource access matters as much as method.

Starting fear:

- being declared useless and sent back to a life with no path.

What he cannot do at the start:

- draw qi into the body;
- use qi in action;
- prove gray response has value;
- challenge hierarchy;
- safely use residual qi;
- understand alchemy residue;
- gain elder attention.

## Large Trend Pressure

青岚宗 is resource-conscious and hierarchy-driven.

The sect keeps trial workers because they are cheap labor with a small chance of yielding overlooked value.

陆沉 cannot stop or change this system. He can only survive inside it and find a crack.

## Growth Direction

By the end of the first major arc, 陆沉 should become:

- a low-level named disciple who has discovered a repeatable method for using discarded residual qi carefully;
- someone who understands that sect hierarchy is not fair but has predictable cost logic;
- someone who can preserve, test, and reuse weak advantages without exposing them too early.

Not by becoming:

- a suddenly recognized genius;
- an elder's chosen disciple in chapter one;
- a system user;
- a treasure inheritor;
- a face-slapping power fantasy too early.

Core growth verb:

```text
survive -> sense -> preserve -> test -> use -> conceal -> repeat
```

## Growth Stages

### Stage 1: Keep the Spark

Current capability:

- can faintly preserve gray response after contact with testing stone.

Primary pressure:

- trial-worker status, labor, hunger, lack of status, and incomplete method.

Lesson to learn:

- first survive and keep the gray response from disappearing; do not rush to prove anything.

Usable leverage gained:

- continued gray response;
- access to 药渣房 or other discarded residues;
- knowledge that ignored waste may not be fully dead.

Cost paid:

- low status;
- labor;
- sleep loss;
- body fatigue;
- social humiliation.

Still cannot do:

- absorb qi safely;
- leave measurable trace on testing paper;
- convince others;
- use qi outwardly.

Forbidden jump:

- immediate breakthrough or treasure identification.

Typical scene pressure:

- dirty work, time pressure, weak residue, body exhaustion, risk of being dismissed.

### Stage 2: Distinguish Dead From Not Dead

Current capability:

- can notice which discarded residues still carry a faint trace.

Primary pressure:

- must sort dangerous / useless / faintly useful waste without alerting others.

Lesson to learn:

- usefulness is not the same as safety.

Usable leverage gained:

- a method for selecting low-risk residual materials;
- a small repeatable cultivation resource.

Cost paid:

- injured hands;
- fatigue;
- possible poisoning / contamination risk;
- suspicion from other trial workers.

Still cannot do:

- convert residue into stable cultivation quickly;
- explain his perception publicly.

Forbidden jump:

- refining pills or creating artifacts.

### Stage 3: First Trace

Current capability:

- can use tiny residual qi under controlled conditions.

Primary pressure:

-百日 trial deadline and measurable testing paper requirement.

Lesson to learn:

- preserving qi is different from letting qi enter the body.

Usable leverage gained:

- first measurable trace on testing paper or equivalent proof.

Cost paid:

- dangerous exhaustion;
- risk of revealing abnormality;
- debt to a minor helper or obligation from an overseer.

Still cannot do:

- stable cultivation;
- outer disciple status beyond minimal acceptance.

Forbidden jump:

- defeating formal outer disciples.

### Stage 4: Concealed Method

Current capability:

- can repeat a tiny residue-based cultivation process.

Primary pressure:

- others begin to notice that he is not collapsing like most trial workers.

Lesson to learn:

- an advantage must be hidden until it becomes defensible.

Usable leverage gained:

- repeatable low-grade cultivation crack;
- possible named-disciple transition;
- one limited relationship or debt.

Cost paid:

- suspicion;
- forced work assignment;
- possible confrontation with peer or low-level manager.

Still cannot do:

- openly claim special status;
- challenge official resource hierarchy.

Forbidden jump:

- major elder recognition.

## Growth Asset Ladder

```text
gray response preserved
-> access to discarded residue
-> ability to distinguish faint residue
-> low-risk residue selection
-> first measurable qi trace
-> concealment method
-> minimal status change
-> one usable relationship / debt
```

## Invisible Hand Rule

Allowed:

- place 陆沉 at low-status but resource-adjacent labor sites;
- let ignored scraps contain tiny usable residue;
- make costs real and bodily;
- force him to choose between survival and practice;
- let others overlook him because his value is low and unproven.

Not allowed:

- give him rare treasure early;
- make a senior immediately identify his value;
- let him skip from gray response to stable qi entry;
- solve trial deadline by coincidence;
- make support characters exist only to hand him advantage.

## Chapter 1 Contract

Chapter 1 should only establish:

- gray response appears;
- sect does not value it;
- 陆沉 enters trial-worker status;
- 药渣房 becomes the likely first crack;
- he preserves the gray response overnight without using it.

Chapter 1 must not establish:

- full goldfinger function;
- qi absorption;
- measurable progress;
- elder attention;
- major conflict escalation.
