# Base Settings

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Canon status: approved test setup for workflow regression.

## Genre Foundation

Traditional xianxia with a low-status sect entry opening.

The opening should feel like a harsh cultivation world where resource allocation, spirit-root recognition, sect cost logic, bodily limits, and social hierarchy all matter.

## Core Operating Logic

The story world runs on cultivation resources and sect hierarchy.

A person without recognized spirit-root value is not treated as a future cultivator. They may still be used as low-cost labor if the sect can extract value with little risk.

The protagonist's first opportunity must come from an overlooked crack in the system, not from a major treasure, elder favor, or system interface.

## Spirit Root / Qi-Sense Rules

The sect's testing stone primarily recognizes stable five-element spirit-root response.

Recognized responses:

- clear five-element light: can enter outer sect depending on strength;
- weak five-element light: lower outer placement if age is acceptable;
- gray response / gray echo: not a recognized five-element root, not enough for outer disciple status;
- no response: rejected.

Gray response is not treated as talent by the sect.

Gray response may indicate sensitivity to residual qi, old qi traces, degraded spiritual materials, exhausted artifacts, damaged arrays, herb residue, furnace ash, or other low-value spiritual leftovers.

At opening stage, gray response can only be preserved and faintly sensed. It cannot directly absorb qi, refine treasure, heal wounds, attack, identify all materials, or produce visible power.

## Cultivation Model

Terms:

- qi sense: faintly sensing qi or residual qi;
- qi drawing: guiding external qi toward the body;
- qi entering the body: retaining qi in the body long enough to leave a measurable trace;
- stable cultivation: not available to the protagonist at opening.

Orthodox sect methods are more complete than village fragments. A complete basic method gives direction, circulation order, warning points, and safe stopping points. A village fragment can create sensation but often causes chest blockage, scattered breath, and fatigue.

Cultivation consumes:

- food;
- sleep;
- body strength;
- quiet time;
- mental focus;
- access to qi;
- correct method.

A mortal body cannot do heavy labor, starve, lose sleep every night, and still advance rapidly without mechanism. Any growth must be small, costly, and tied to a plausible resource crack.

## Resource Model

Primary resources:

- spirit roots;
- complete cultivation methods;
- spirit rice;
- spirit stones;
- pills;
- herbs;
- spirit spring water;
- furnace residue;
- discarded spiritual materials;
- senior attention;
- sect status.

Resource hierarchy:

- inner sect receives high-quality resources;
- outer sect receives controlled low-level resources;
- named disciples receive limited access;
- official servants /杂役 receive survival-level support and occasional scraps;
- trial workers /试役 receive coarse grain, lodging, low-value instruction, and unstable access to scraps.

Spirit rice is not supplied to trial workers.

Trial workers may receive coarse grain and vegetable soup. As a pressure-management tactic, the sect may provide extremely diluted spiritual leftovers such as spirit-congee pot water, but not as formal cultivation support.

Discarded materials may still contain residual qi, but recognized disciples usually ignore them because they are impure, dangerous, time-consuming, and low yield.

## Sect / Institution Model

Sect: 青岚宗.

青岚宗 is not a top sect. It has enough structure to run tests, outer houses, servant labor, and low-level resource processing, but it is resource-conscious and cost-sensitive.

Hierarchy:

1. elders / hall heads;
2. inner disciples;
3. outer upper house;
4. outer lower house;
5. named disciples /记名弟子;
6. official servants /杂役;
7. trial workers /试役;
8. rejected candidates.

试役制度 exists because it lets the sect extract short-term labor from weak, unstable, or ambiguous candidates while preserving a small chance of finding overlooked usefulness.

Trial workers are not outer disciples and should not be treated as equals by official outer disciples.

Trial workers can be dismissed after the trial period with minimal cost.

## Opening Location Rules

Opening spaces:

- mountain gate;
- spirit-testing stone area;
- trial-worker waiting shed;
- back-mountain servant compound;
- water room;
- firewood shed;
- furnace-residue /药渣房.

Restricted spaces such as inner mountain, scripture hall, elder hall, and real alchemy rooms should not be accessed in chapter one.

## Labor / Survival Model

Trial workers eat enough to survive and work, not enough to cultivate comfortably.

Labor is oppressive but not instantly lethal.

Possible trial labor:

- carrying tail spring water;
- sorting furnace waste;
- cleaning low-risk alchemy residue;
- chopping wet wood;
- sweeping outer areas;
- moving low-grade materials.

Work should compete with cultivation time and body strength.

Growth is possible only if the protagonist finds a narrow resource crack and manages time, body, and risk carefully.

## Protagonist Specialness Boundary

陆沉 can preserve a faint gray response after contact with the testing stone.

At first, he can only:

- sense that the gray response did not disappear;
- notice extremely weak residual qi when directly exposed or physically close;
- distinguish “dead” waste from “not fully dead” waste after repeated contact and effort.

He cannot yet:

- absorb residual qi safely;
- refine pills;
- identify high-level treasures;
- fight with qi;
- convince the sect of his value;
- bypass the trial period;
- attract elder attention.

## Social Behavior Rules

Hierarchy changes behavior.

A newly accepted outer disciple will usually be led away and will not waste time continuing to mock a trial worker unless there is direct later contact.

A low-status protagonist from a survival background should not show naive generosity. Any help to others should be limited, practical, and tied to survival, work-sharing, debt, or future safety.

High-status actors should exploit lower-status actors through ordinary institutional advantage, not cartoonish harassment.

## Opening Chapter Constraints

Chapter one may prove:

- 陆沉 is not fully without response;
- gray response is not valued by the sect;
- trial-worker status is low and exploitative;
- 青岚宗's resource hierarchy is harsh;
- 陆沉 has the will and survival sense to accept a narrow chance.

Chapter one may hint:

- gray response can persist;
- discarded materials or residue may matter later;
- 药渣房 may contain the first usable crack.

Chapter one must not prove:

- the full goldfinger function;
- rapid cultivation success;
- elder interest;
- true treasure discovery;
- major sect conspiracy;
- protagonist victory over the hierarchy.

## Forbidden Setting Moves

- No system interface.
- No random treasure in ordinary wooden token.
- No elder immediately recognizing protagonist's hidden value.
- No starving heavy-labor miracle breakthrough.
- No official outer disciple lingering in the testing line to bully a trial worker after status separation.
- No document/process climax.
- No rare resource without source, price, danger, debt, or pursuit.
