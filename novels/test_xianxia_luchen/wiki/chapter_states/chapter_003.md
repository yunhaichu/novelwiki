# Chapter 003 State

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Chapter title: 一勺水

Canon status: approved test draft state.

## Chapter Function

Second labor chapter.

Turns gray response from a passive sensation into a scarce, costly judgment aid. It also establishes that peer interactions in trial-worker space are transactional, not sentimental.

## Confirmed Events

- 方远 asks 陆沉 to inspect his injured hand.
- 陆沉 warns 方远 to avoid dark-red medicine mud because his hand is broken.
- 宋满 asks for guidance and is told to handle only black ash and broken porcelain first.
- 马全 brings an extra bucket of exploded-furnace waste.
- Exploded-furnace waste requires four sorting categories, including separate blue-black furnace-lining fragments.
- 宋满 nearly misplaces a furnace-lining fragment; 陆沉 warns him once.
- Later 宋满 accidentally drops blue-black fragments into medicine mud.
- 陆沉 tells 宋满 to retrieve them before 马全 seals the bucket.
- 马全 catches the mistake.
- 宋满 admits fault.
- 陆沉 points out that the fragments were retrieved before being sent away, reducing the punishment.
- 宋满 loses half a meal; 陆沉 and 方远 are not punished.
- 陆沉 suffers chest tightness and fatigue from overusing gray response.
- 宋满 gives 陆沉 a piece of bitter tongue root to suppress nausea, creating a small debt.
- 陆沉 realizes gray response must be used sparingly.

## Protagonist State

Growth stage: between Stage 1 and early Stage 2.

Current capability:

- can preserve gray response;
- can sometimes detect not-fully-dead or dangerous residue cues;
- can use it as a judgment aid when ordinary senses are insufficient.

New lesson:

- gray response is costly and cannot be used continuously.
- It should be saved for key decisions.

Limits:

- cannot absorb qi;
- cannot refine residue;
- cannot leave measurable trace;
- cannot use gray response as a full detection ability;
- use causes fatigue / chest tightness.

Cost paid:

- physical exhaustion;
- chest tightness;
- risk of 马全 noticing his unusual judgment;
- involvement in 宋满's mistake.

Usable leverage gained:

- better understanding of gray response cost;
- 方远's practical dependence;
- 宋满's small debt via bitter tongue root;
- continued credibility in 药渣房.

## Organization State

药渣房:

- can receive exploded-furnace waste requiring more complex sorting.
- mistakes cause meal penalties or extra work.

马全:

- punishes but does not overpunish when work is salvaged.
- still values completed work more than emotional discipline.

## Key Object / Resource State

- Exploded-furnace waste: more complex and dangerous than normal waste.
- Blue-black furnace-lining fragments: must be separated; misplacement causes rework/punishment.
- Bitter tongue root: mundane herb carried by 宋满, can suppress nausea but is not spiritual.
- Gray response: confirmed as scarce and costly; use must be conserved.
