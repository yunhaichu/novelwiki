# Style

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Canon status: approved test setup.

## Prose Direction

Traditional xianxia with grounded physical detail and restrained tension.

The prose should feel simple, readable, and concrete. Avoid ornate mysticism before the world earns it.

## Opening Texture

Preferred details:

- rain, stone, mud, cloth, wood, food, tools;
- visible hierarchy through movement and separation;
- resource differences shown through ration, lodging, access, and labor;
- spiritual abnormality as faint sensation, not glowing spectacle.

## Language Rules

Use plain Chinese narrative.

Avoid:

- over-explaining rules in manual form;
- repeated slogans about destiny;
- AI-flavored summary sentences;
- excessive metaphors;
- modern office phrasing;
- system-interface language;
- dramatic inner monologue that states the theme directly.

## Dialogue Rules

Characters speak from status, task, and immediate pressure.

- 执事: rule-based, brief, not emotional.
- 韩管事: practical, cost-aware, dry.
- 陆沉: short, practical questions; little self-explanation.
- 赵明川: status-conscious before testing; controlled after acceptance.

## Goldfinger Presentation

The gray response should be presented as faint, bodily, uncertain, and limited.

Chapter one may show that the response persists.

Chapter one must not show a full function, active detection network, visible power, or object activation.

## Anti-AI Notes

Avoid sentences like:

- 命运的齿轮开始转动。
- 他不知道的是……
- 一切都不一样了。
- 这只是开始。
- 他终于明白了什么叫修仙界的残酷。

Show through action instead.
