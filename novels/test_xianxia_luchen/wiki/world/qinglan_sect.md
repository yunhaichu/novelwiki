# Qinglan Sect

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Canon status: approved test setup.

## Public Legitimacy

青岚宗 presents itself as an orthodox cultivation sect that gives qualified youths a path into cultivation.

It claims selection is based on spirit-root response, age, discipline, and ability to endure hardship.

## Real Interests

- preserve scarce resources for candidates with clear value;
- maintain enough low-level labor for outer operations;
- avoid wasting spirit rice, pills, and teaching time on candidates unlikely to advance;
- keep a small screening channel for ambiguous or marginal candidates who may prove useful.

## Hierarchy

1. elders / hall heads;
2. inner disciples;
3. outer upper house;
4. outer lower house;
5. named disciples / 记名弟子;
6. official servants / 杂役;
7. trial workers / 试役;
8. rejected candidates.

## Trial Worker System

试役 is not outer disciple status.

The sect uses 试役 to process candidates with weak, unstable, or non-standard responses.

Benefits to the sect:

- obtains low-cost labor for 100 days;
- risks little formal resource expenditure;
- preserves chance of finding rare overlooked usefulness;
- can dismiss failures with minimal obligation.

What 试役 receives:

- lodging in back-mountain servant compound;
- coarse food;
- low-level labor assignment;
- basic instruction once every ten days;
- possible conversion to 记名弟子 if measurable qi trace appears.

What 试役 does not receive:

- spirit rice allocation;
- outer disciple clothing / rooms / status;
- regular teaching;
- protection from ordinary low-level exploitation;
- free access to scripture hall, alchemy hall, or inner mountain.

## Resource Logic

青岚宗 treats resources by value tier.

High-value resources are protected and allocated upward.

Low-value residue is often discarded, diluted, assigned to servants, or used in low-status maintenance work.

The sect does not yet know that 陆沉 can detect a special value in some residual traces.

## Common Low-Level Work Sites

- 水房: carries tail spring water. Labor heavy; weak spiritual residue possible but highly diluted.
- 柴房: chops wet wood. Labor heavy; little cultivation opportunity.
- 药渣房: sorts low-risk furnace waste and failed medicine residue. Dirty and risky; possible residual qi traces.
- outer sweeping / carrying work: low status, public humiliation, little spiritual value.

## Behavior Under Pressure

青岚宗 usually chooses the lowest-cost response first.

It will not deploy elders or high-level actors for a trial worker unless clear value, danger, scandal, or contamination risk appears.

Low managers may exploit trial workers through assignments, ration control, and task records, but they should not behave like cartoon villains without cost logic.

## Embodied Agents

- mountain-gate gray-clothed testing执事: applies selection rules.
- guarding outer disciples: maintain order and separate accepted candidates from rejected ones.
- 韩管事: controls early trial-worker assignment and day-to-day survival pressure.

## Cannot Admit

青岚宗 will not publicly say that 试役 is cheap labor.

It packages the system as hardship screening, discipline, and giving marginal candidates one last chance.

## Opening Use

In chapter one, 青岚宗 should appear through:

- testing stone rules;
- outer disciple / trial worker separation;
- food/resource distinctions;
- assignment to labor sites;
- institutional indifference rather than dramatic malice.
