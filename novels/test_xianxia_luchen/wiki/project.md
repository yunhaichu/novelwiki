# Project

Novel ID: `test_xianxia_luchen`

Status: test novel / workflow regression case.

## Type Promise

Traditional xianxia / mortal-to-cultivator opening.

The story follows a low-status protagonist entering a cultivation sect through a marginal, exploitative trial-worker channel rather than a glorious talent route.

## Core Reader Promise

- Low starting point.
- Clear cultivation hierarchy.
- Sect resource scarcity.
- Weak but real protagonist specialness.
- Slow, costly, reusable growth.
- The protagonist finds usable cracks in a harsh cultivation system rather than receiving a large treasure early.

## Protagonist

Name: 陆沉

Starting identity: fourteen-year-old medicine apprentice from 白石镇.

Opening status: not outer disciple; accepted as `试役` because of weak gray response on the spirit-testing stone.

## Core Contradiction

The cultivation world values clear spirit roots, resources, hierarchy, and cost-efficiency.

陆沉 has no recognized standard spirit root, but he can preserve and later sense extremely weak residual qi that others ignore.

## Story Engine

```text
sect hierarchy and resource scarcity
-> low-status trial labor
-> discarded / low-value cultivation residues
-> protagonist detects a narrow opportunity
-> small gain with body/time/social cost
-> repeated use under pressure
-> gradual status change
```

## What This Story Is Not

- Not instant treasure inheritance.
- Not system-interface cultivation.
- Not office-process fiction in sect clothing.
- Not early face-slapping against major elders.
- Not protagonist solving sect-level conflict in the opening.
- Not a story where documents, ledgers, or tokens drive the plot.
