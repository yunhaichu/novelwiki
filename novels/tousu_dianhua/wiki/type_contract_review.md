- Reader promise: PASS. Status reversal, justice, truth, and survival security are concrete reader rewards.

- Chapter 1-3 expectation: PASS. Chapter 1, chapter 3, and volume 1 delivery targets are specific.
- Genre blend control: PASS. Suspense supports proof and leverage rather than replacing the rebirth/status line.
- False promise prevention: PASS. It explicitly rejects stock, real estate, lottery, and easy wealth drift.
