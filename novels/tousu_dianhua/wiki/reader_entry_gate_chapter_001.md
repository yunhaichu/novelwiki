# Reader Entry Gate: Chapter 001

## Chapter / opening unit

Chapter 001.

## Target reader

Urban rebirth and workplace counterattack reader who expects immediate reversal opportunity, career risk, practical leverage, and a clear reason to continue.

## Primary genre promise

Urban rebirth / workplace counterattack: a failed worker returns to a decisive work moment and uses future memory plus procedural leverage to change status, risk, and justice outcomes.

## First 500 words must make clear

1. Chen Jiming is the protagonist.
2. He has returned to a past customer-service shift before a decisive complaint call.
3. He needs today's clean quality-inspection result and transfer-to-permanent bonus for his mother's surgery deposit.
4. The incoming call is one he remembers mishandling in the previous life.
5. Procedure says transfer the call away, but future memory says doing so will help cause disaster.
6. The reader should continue because his first choice will immediately risk his job and change the old outcome.

## Reader should remember only these 3-4 things

1. A reborn customer-service worker is waiting for a call that once ruined him.
2. He needs his transfer bonus for his mother's surgery.
3. Procedure tells him to transfer the complaint away.
4. This time he chooses to escalate it and creates a new record.

## Information allowed now

- Customer-service hall and quality inspection.
- Chen Jiming's transfer-to-permanent pressure.
- Mother's surgery deposit pressure.
- Caller Liang Guilan and the elevator complaint.
- The cold emergency-risk clause he uses to escalate the call.
- Zhao Shuhe and Luo Qing as immediate workplace witnesses.

## Information delayed

- Full future hearing.
- Full accident chain.
- Feng Yao's full identity and motive.
- Company acquisition background.
- All later complaint records.

## Terms forbidden in opening

- Do not create new system labels.
- Do not use abstract labels such as fate, chain, hidden network, old timeline, or rebirth system.
- Do not name a conspiracy yet.

## Worldbuilding limit

Only explain the customer-service rule needed for Chen's immediate choice: ordinary transfer vs emergency-risk escalation.

## Backstory limit

Use only brief memory fragments tied to the call, his mother's surgery deposit, and his later scapegoat fate. Do not explain the full previous life.

## Opening action / pressure

Chen Jiming sits under live quality inspection as the old call is about to connect.

## Opening image or object

The call-center screen shows:

```text
来电人：梁桂兰
事项：社区电梯困人，物业无人处理
系统建议：转物业平台
质检风险：不得越权承诺
```

## Protagonist visible choice

He refuses the ordinary transfer path and uses an emergency-risk clause to create a formal escalation record.

## Visible cost or consequence

Zhao Shuhe notices and warns him that his transfer form may not be signed if he continues.

## End-of-chapter question

What did Liang Guilan mean when she said someone had already been carried out of the same elevator last month, and why was that record handled by the future executive who later blamed Chen?

## One-sentence reader recap target

A reborn customer-service worker risks his transfer bonus for his mother's surgery by escalating the complaint call he once mishandled, and the caller gives him a clue from the old timeline that points above his level.

## Hard failure risks

- protagonist as job function: avoid by giving Chen immediate desire and pressure.
- too many mysteries before care: keep only the phone call and elevator complaint.
- setting correct but unreadable: keep workplace rules tied to the active choice.
