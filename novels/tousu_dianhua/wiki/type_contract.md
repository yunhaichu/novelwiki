# Type Contract: 投诉电话

## Project title

投诉电话

## Primary genre

Urban rebirth / workplace counterattack.

## Secondary genre

Light realistic suspense.

## Primary reader promise

A failed ordinary worker returns to a decisive earlier moment and uses future knowledge, professional rules, and social leverage to reverse personal ruin while climbing out of a low-status workplace position.

## Main payoff type

```text
status / justice / truth / survival security
```

## What reader expects by chapter 1

- Chen Jiming has returned to a past workday before a decisive phone call.
- He remembers that following procedure in his previous life caused disaster and later destroyed his career and family.
- He must choose between protecting his transfer-to-permanent chance and intervening in a dangerous complaint.
- His first change creates both a visible risk and a new clue.

## What reader expects by chapter 3

- The complaint is not an isolated service issue.
- Chen Jiming's altered action opens a path into a larger responsibility chain.
- His workplace position is more dangerous but also more useful than before.
- Someone in the company notices that he is no longer following the old pattern.

## What reader expects by volume 1 end

- Chen Jiming prevents or alters the first elevator-related disaster.
- He gains leverage inside the customer-service outsourcing company.
- A hidden record links the old complaint chain to a future executive who once blamed him.
- His mother's medical-money pressure remains active and ties personal survival to workplace action.

## Opening must show

- The call is about to connect.
- Chen Jiming needs the transfer-to-permanent bonus for his mother's surgery deposit.
- The call is one he remembers from his previous life.
- Procedure and rescue are in direct conflict.

## Opening must not hide

- The protagonist is reborn.
- The phone call matters.
- The immediate cost of noncompliance is job loss or transfer failure.
- The primary genre is urban rebirth with status and justice stakes, not pure office routine.

## Information that can be delayed

- The full hearing in the previous life.
- The entire company conspiracy.
- The complete accident chain.
- The identity and motive of the future executive.
- Detailed platform rules beyond what creates the immediate choice.

## Forbidden false promises

- Do not promise stock speculation, real estate windfall, lottery, or easy wealth.
- Do not promise pure detective fiction where official proof is the only payoff.
- Do not make the rebirth function like omnipotence.
- Do not make workplace procedure the main reward unless it changes status, leverage, risk, or justice.

## If genre-blended, which genre is primary

Urban rebirth / workplace counterattack is primary.

## How secondary genre supports primary genre

The suspense line gives Chen Jiming proof paths, hidden responsibility chains, and future knowledge tests. It should support his status reversal and justice pursuit rather than replace them.

## How the ending should pay the primary promise

Volume 1 should pay by improving Chen Jiming's leverage and public/professional position while exposing a responsibility chain. The truth payoff should also change his status and future options.

## Type drift risks

1. Turning into procedural customer-service workplace routine.
2. Turning into pure conspiracy mystery without visible career and family stakes.
3. Turning into easy rebirth wish fulfillment where future memory solves problems without cost.

## One-sentence reader contract

A failed customer-service supervisor returns to the phone call that once ruined him and must use future memory and procedural loopholes to save lives, protect his mother, and climb out of the system that made him a scapegoat.
