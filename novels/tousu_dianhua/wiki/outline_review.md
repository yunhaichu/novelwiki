# Outline Review: 投诉电话

## Decision

PASS.

## Main reason

The outline serves the type contract, supports a clear reader-entry chapter 1, and defines urban rebirth progress through status, leverage, proof, and family survival rather than through easy wealth or generic workplace routine.

## Scores

- Type contract alignment: PASS. Urban rebirth / workplace counterattack remains primary; suspense supports proof and leverage.
- Reader entry feasibility: PASS. Chapter 1 can show protagonist, call, surgery deposit, remembered disaster, procedure conflict, and immediate choice without requiring wiki knowledge.
- Hook strength: PASS. The same complaint call from the previous life creates immediate pressure and recognizability.
- Goal clarity: PASS. Prevent the incident while protecting job conversion and medical deposit.
- Relationship tension: PASS. Zhao Shuhe, Luo Qing, Liang Guilan, and future Feng Yao create layered pressure.
- Main genre promise relevance: PASS. Progress is status, leverage, record access, risk control, and justice.
- Consequence / escalation capacity: PASS. The first altered choice can trigger quality-inspection risk, caller trust, record access, and supervisor attention.
- Expansion capacity: PASS. Future complaint records can become proof chains and workplace leverage.
- Consistency risk: manageable. Future memory must remain partial and costly.

## Required fixes if REVISE

None.
