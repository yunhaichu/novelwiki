# Cosmic Civilization Arena

Use this prompt for macro-scale stories: cosmic cultivation, interstellar fantasy, multi-civilization war, modern-to-cosmic entry, xianxia + technology + magic hybrid settings.

Purpose: prevent small-stage stories when the target is a grand webnovel world.

## Core Rule

Do not build a macro story from a village, sect, city, or single country upward.

First define the civilization arena.

The reader may only see a small crack in chapter one, but the author must know the large arena from the start.

## Hard Checks

Mark `REVISE` if:

- the world is only a sect, city, island, or small country while the intended story needs cosmic scope;
- civilizations do not have resource motives;
- Earth is only the protagonist's hometown and has no narrative value;
- all power systems are decorative labels rather than different energy-use routes;
- the large arena cannot support academy arcs, exploration arcs, resource arcs, political arcs, war arcs, and home-defense arcs;
- chapter one tries to explain the entire arena instead of showing a small crack.
