# Reader Entry Gate Prompt

Use this prompt after `wiki/base_settings.md` passes review and before any opening chapter brief or opening draft.

The purpose is to test whether a first-time target reader can enter the story without reading the wiki.

Do not write prose.
Do not write a chapter brief.
Do not solve the plot.
Do not explain the whole setting.

## Required Inputs

Read:

- current novel `wiki/project.md`
- current novel `wiki/base_settings.md`
- current novel `wiki/style.md` if present
- current novel `wiki/name_registry.md` if present
- protagonist character file if present
- relevant pending setting gaps
- `governance/base_settings_review.md`

## Use When

Use this for:

- chapter 1;
- chapters 1-3 as an opening unit;
- a new volume opening;
- a new major arc opening;
- the first appearance of a major new system, world area, genre mechanism, or protagonist reset.

## Hard Principle

Reader entry outranks setting completeness.

A chapter that is correct to the wiki but not enterable by a first-time target reader must be marked `REVISE` before drafting.

## Review Rules

Mark `REVISE` if:

- a first-time reader cannot identify the protagonist in the first 500 words;
- the immediate trouble is unclear;
- the protagonist has no visible immediate desire;
- the cost of inaction is unclear;
- the reader itch is only abstract mystery and not tied to protagonist pressure;
- the opening requires too many names, systems, rules, backstory items, or unresolved mysteries before caring about the protagonist;
- the one-sentence reader recap cannot be stated in ordinary language;
- any blocking gap remains unresolved.

## Drafting Constraint

If this gate returns `REVISE`, do not create an opening chapter brief yet.
