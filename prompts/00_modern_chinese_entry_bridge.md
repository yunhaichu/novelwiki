# Modern Chinese Entry Bridge

Use this prompt for modern Chinese protagonist entry stories: transmigration, contact with high civilization, Earth-to-cosmic entry, future transfer, or modern person entering xianxia / magic / interstellar systems.

Purpose: preserve reader self-insertion and modern-person viewpoint.

## Core Rule

The protagonist should feel like a reader's modern compatriot entering a vast world.

Do not make the protagonist instantly become a native of the new system.

## Hard Checks

Mark `REVISE` if:

- the protagonist could be any generic fantasy native;
- modern identity disappears after chapter one;
- modern knowledge is either useless flavor or overpowered instant solution;
- inner monologue lacks reader-laoxiang feeling;
- the protagonist's entry into the macro world is pure coincidence;
- the Earth emotional anchor is missing;
- modern jokes replace plot instead of sharpening viewpoint.
