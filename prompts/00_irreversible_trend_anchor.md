# Irreversible Trend Anchor

Use this once during new-novel setup, before chapter planning and before ordinary event generation.

Purpose: prevent the story from starting with a random event and then forcing characters to perform inside it.

## Core Principle

```text
Author controls irreversible world trend.
Author controls protagonist final form.
Characters control local choices.
Plot emerges when local choices are pulled back into the irreversible trend.
```

No chapter may be designed before this anchor exists.

## Checks

Mark `REVISE` if:

- the world trend is just a background setting, not an irreversible movement;
- the protagonist final form is only `becomes stronger`;
- world trend and protagonist final form do not force each other;
- early chapters can be replaced by random incidents without damaging the long story;
- the anchor cannot produce stage pressure for volume and chapter design.
