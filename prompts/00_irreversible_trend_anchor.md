# Irreversible Trend Anchor

Use this once during new-novel setup, before chapter planning and before ordinary event generation.

Purpose: prevent the story from starting with a random event and then forcing characters to perform inside it.

## Core Principle

```text
Author controls irreversible world / civilization trend.
Author controls protagonist final form.
Characters control local choices.
Plot emerges when local choices are pulled back into the irreversible trend.
```

For macro modern-to-cosmic stories, the anchor has three lines:

```text
cosmic civilization irreversible trend
Earth civilization irreversible pressure
protagonist irreversible final form
```

No chapter may be designed before this anchor exists.

## 6. Hard Constraints

Do not write chapters that only solve local incidents.

Every important chapter must advance at least one:
- world / civilization irreversible trend;
- Earth civilization pressure or status when relevant;
- protagonist final form;
- coupling among the above.

Every important commercial webnovel chapter must also deliver visible reader pleasure.

## Checks

Mark `REVISE` if:

- the world / civilization trend is just a background setting, not an irreversible movement;
- Earth is only hometown nostalgia in a macro Earth-entry story;
- the protagonist final form is only `becomes stronger`;
- world trend, Earth pressure, and protagonist final form do not force each other;
- early chapters can be replaced by random incidents without damaging the long story;
- the anchor cannot produce stage pressure for volume and chapter design;
- the anchor cannot produce visible reader pleasure without abandoning the main trend.
