# Earth Civilization Value

Use this prompt for modern-Earth-to-cosmic stories.

Purpose: make Earth more than the protagonist's hometown. Earth must have narrative value, vulnerability, and a reason to be observed, exploited, protected, or contested by higher civilizations.

## Core Rule

Earth cannot be merely weak.

Earth must be low-level in energy use but special in a way that can matter later.

## Hard Checks

Mark `REVISE` if:

- Earth is only used for nostalgia;
- Earth has no unique value beyond being weak;
- the protagonist could be from any low-level planet without changing the story;
- high civilizations have no reason to observe or debate Earth;
- Earth stakes are only abstract and do not touch the protagonist's family, home, school, work, city, or country;
- Earth becomes important too early through author declaration rather than earned reevaluation.
