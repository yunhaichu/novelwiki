# Opening Chapter Brief Prompt

Use this prompt after `prompts/00_reader_entry_gate.md` returns `ALLOW` for chapter 1, chapters 1-3, a new volume opening, or a major new arc opening.

The purpose is to define the opening chapter's pressure in a draftable form without overloading it with normal long-form planning fields.

Do not write prose.
Do not expand the setting encyclopedia.
Do not solve the whole incident.

## Required Inputs

Read:

- current novel `wiki/project.md`
- current novel `wiki/base_settings.md`
- current novel `wiki/style.md`
- current novel `wiki/name_registry.md`
- protagonist character file
- approved reader entry gate
- relevant pending setting gaps

Output only the opening chapter brief:

```text
-

## Drafting Constraints

-
```

## Rules

- The protagonist must appear as a person under pressure, not a job label, observer, or process executor only.
- The opening must show one active choice.
- The chapter must create a visible consequence of that choice.
- The reader itch must be tied to the protagonist's risk or desire.
- Do not start with world explanation.
- Do not introduce too many proper nouns or system labels.
- Do not let mystery replace stake.
- Do not let setting wonder replace protagonist situation.
- Do not turn process details into a manual.
- Use the base settings as boundaries, not as exposition.

## Review Trigger

If the brief cannot state a one-sentence reader recap target in ordinary language, mark the brief as failed and return to the reader entry gate.
