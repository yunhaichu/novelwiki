# Unified Power Logic

Use this prompt when a story contains multiple power systems: cultivation, magic, technology, psychic systems, biological systems, divine systems, or higher-dimensional rules.

Purpose: prevent power systems from becoming decorative labels. At high levels, different systems should be different routes to energy, law, information, and life transformation.

## Core Rule

Do not treat cultivation, magic, and technology as unrelated costumes.

They must be comparable through a shared underlying model.

## 8. Chapter Use

In chapters, power logic should appear as:

- visible effect;
- concrete cost;
- measurable difference;
- tactical choice;
- social consequence;
- resource constraint.

Do not explain the whole model in prose.

## Hard Checks

Mark `REVISE` if:

- power systems are only names with no shared logic;
- modern technology is either useless or unrealistically superior without cost;
- cultivation is reduced to generic realm names;
- magic is reduced to arbitrary spell names;
- cross-system understanding makes the protagonist instantly solve everything;
- the power model cannot generate tactical scenes, resource competition, academy conflict, and civilization war.
