# Modern Common Reference

Use this reference for modern, urban, workplace, business, school, family, platform, and realistic rebirth projects.

This file is a reference setting base. It is not canon for any novel.

## Identity And Authority

Common roles:

- ordinary employee: executes assigned work and has limited cross-team authority.
- intern: assists and observes; low formal authority.
- team lead: distributes small-team tasks; usually lacks budget or personnel decision power.
- supervisor / manager: manages delivery, progress, evaluation, and limited external coordination.
- director / founder: controls larger strategy, funding, and major resource allocation, but remains constrained by cash flow, market, law, reputation, and internal politics.
- investor / client decision-maker / platform operator: controls access to money, orders, traffic, evaluation, or acceptance.
- teacher / doctor / lawyer / police / community worker: acts within professional, procedural, legal, and institutional boundaries.

Authority rule:

A character's role should define what they can request, block, ignore, approve, conceal, or expose. A low-authority character may still influence events through evidence, timing, relationships, public pressure, technical skill, or rule knowledge, but should not directly command high-level resources without support.

## Organization And Process

Common organizations:

- company: employment, business operation, evaluation, resource allocation.
- school: education, ranking, assessment, recommendation, discipline.
- hospital: treatment, cost, qualification, life-and-death pressure.
- bank / lender: account, loan, credit, mortgage, cash-flow pressure.
- platform company: traffic, account rules, recommendation, ban, monetization.
- community / property management: local coordination, notice, access, conflict.
- police station / court / regulator: public order, evidence, procedure, judgment, enforcement.
- media account / social platform: exposure, rumor, public pressure, reversal.
- supplier / client company / channel partner: order, price, delivery, acceptance, relationship.

Common processes:

- onboarding: needs identity material and role confirmation.
- performance review: needs targets, records, evaluator, and consequence.
- contract signing: needs parties, terms, seal, signature, or electronic confirmation.
- payment approval: needs invoice, contract, acceptance, and permission.
- bidding / procurement: needs qualification, plan, quote, relation, and public or internal process.
- client acceptance: needs deliverable, standard, signature, or record.
- police report / legal process: needs facts, evidence, parties, and procedural path.
- loan approval: needs income, credit, collateral, and risk review.
- account review / appeal: needs rule, record, complaint path, and platform decision.
- public opinion spread: needs source, evidence, emotion, network, and reaction.
- rental handover: needs contract, deposit, key, list, and condition check.

Process rule:

A process result should not appear as magically valid. Name the visible support that makes it valid.

## Resource And Object

Useful modern resources:

- cash / account balance / credit limit
- contract / certificate / ID / credential
- access card / account permission / internal record
- public record / surveillance / audit log
- client list / supplier channel / recommendation slot
- house / shop / office / storage space
- patent / code / file / media evidence
- fan group / community relation / reputation

Resource rule:

A meaningful resource should have a holder, access condition, use, risk, and possible loss.

## Space And Location

Common spaces:

- office, meeting room, warehouse, shop, school, hospital, residential compound, police station, court, station, platform backend, livestream room, rented room.

Space rule:

Access to a space should depend on identity, time, permission, relationship, money, or risk. Restricted spaces need an entry condition and a trace left behind.

## Social Life

Modern social pressure often comes from:

- employment status
- salary, debt, rent, family expectation
- reputation and online visibility
- certification, school ranking, career record
- access to customer, platform, supplier, or relationship network
- procedural helplessness under a larger system

Do not let modern characters speak only in plot explanations. Their dialogue should usually be practical, defensive, impatient, transactional, evasive, or status-aware.

## Genre Guard

Avoid:

- giving low-level roles unlimited authority.
- resolving disputes without evidence, procedure, or visible leverage.
- treating online exposure as instantly decisive without propagation path.
- making money, contract, or permission appear without source.
- using workplace, business, or school process as empty decoration with no consequence.
- replacing social pressure with abstract fate language.
