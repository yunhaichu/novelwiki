# Xianxia Common Reference

Use this reference for xianxia, cultivation, xuanhuan, sect, spiritual resource, realm, and dao-related projects.

This file is a reference setting base. It is not canon for any novel.

## Identity And Authority

Common roles:

- outer disciple: low status, limited access, performs basic tasks or tests.
- inner disciple: higher training access and more visible competition.
- direct disciple: tied to a master, lineage, resource access, and faction attention.
- steward / deacon: manages local tasks, resources, discipline, or registration.
- elder: holds teaching, punishment, resource, or faction authority.
- sect master / clan head: controls major direction, protection, conflict, and allocation.
- rogue cultivator: flexible but lacks institutional protection and stable resources.
- merchant cultivator / auction agent / workshop specialist: controls exchange, information, or craft resources.

Authority rule:

Cultivation status should affect access to techniques, tasks, resources, protection, punishment, public challenge, and who can speak in formal settings. A lower-status character can still act through information, risk, timing, hidden skill, debt, or rule loopholes.

## Organization And Process

Common organizations:

- sect: cultivation organization, identity, protection, rules, resource distribution.
- cultivation clan: bloodline, marriage, inheritance, internal faction.
- market / merchant guild: exchange, information, pricing, risk.
- law enforcement hall: discipline, investigation, punishment.
- mission hall: task issue, reward, risk ranking.
- alchemy hall / artifact hall / formation hall: craft production and technical gate.
- auction house: high-value exchange, public competition, reputation.
- rogue cultivator alliance: temporary cooperation and unstable trust.
- demonic or hostile faction: external pressure; should not be flat evil by default unless the project declares it.

Common processes:

- entry test: needs aptitude, age, background, or sponsor.
- outer / inner assessment: needs task result, cultivation level, contribution, or witness.
- mission claim: needs identity, contribution, risk grade, permission.
- contribution exchange: needs contribution, inventory, and status.
- technique transmission: needs lineage, identity, contribution, trust, or permission.
- secret realm registration: needs quota, cultivation level, sponsor, and risk acceptance.
- sect interrogation: needs evidence, witness, sect rule, and authority.
- arena challenge: needs challenge, witness, stake, and accepted rule.
- apprenticeship: needs aptitude, performance, master willingness, and lineage consequence.
- market exchange: needs spirit stones, goods, reputation, or guarantee.

Process rule:

A sect or cultivation process should create pressure through access, quota, public standing, resource cost, punishment risk, or faction notice.

## Resource And Object

Useful cultivation resources:

- spirit stones / contribution points / mission tokens
- technique slips / manuals / jade slips
- pills / herbs / materials / beast cores
- artifact / talisman / formation plate
- cave dwelling / spiritual field / market stall
- disciple token / sect record / challenge document
- witness testimony / punishment order / quota record

Resource rule:

A resource should have grade, access condition, holder, scarcity, and risk. Do not give rare resources without source, price, debt, theft risk, or later consequence.

## Space And Location

Common spaces:

- outer gate, inner mountain, mission hall, law enforcement hall, market, auction house, training ground, arena, spiritual field, cave dwelling, forbidden area, secret realm entrance.

Space rule:

Cultivation spaces should have access rank, danger level, surveillance, social visibility, or resource logic. Entering a restricted area should leave trace or require cost.

## Power / Cultivation Boundary

Cultivation progress should not be pure level labels. It should change what the character can observe, survive, influence, refine, contest, or conceal.

A power display should show visible result: broken object, changed terrain, injured body, forced retreat, failed defense, resource consumption, public reaction, or rule consequence.

## Genre Guard

Avoid:

- overloading early chapters with realm tables.
- giving resources without source.
- letting sect rules exist only when convenient.
- using humiliation loops without information, leverage, or changed status.
- making elders omniscient or foolish only to serve the plot.
- treating demonic / orthodox labels as complete character motivation.
- explaining rules in manual form instead of showing them through action, denial, reward, punishment, or public reaction.
