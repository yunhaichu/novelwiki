# Failure Case: Type Promise Unclear

## Symptom

The opening contains genre-adjacent material, but the reader cannot tell what kind of story they are entering.

Examples:

- mystery elements without a clear problem;
- fantasy setting without wonder, rule pressure, or growth path;
- workplace material without status, reputation, resource, or procedural risk;
- romance-adjacent dialogue without relationship movement.

## Reject Rule

Mark `REVISE` if the opening does not visibly signal the declared primary genre promise.

## Repair Strategy

Return to the type contract.

Make one primary promise visible on the page through pressure, choice, reward, threat, relationship movement, clue, rule, or cost.

## Expected Decision

```text
REVISE
```
