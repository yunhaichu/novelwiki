# Failure Case: Hook Is Weird Not Compelling

## Symptom

The opening contains a strange image, abnormal object, prophecy, system message, impossible event, or mysterious sentence, but it does not create a reason to continue.

The hook is unusual, but not tied to desire, stake, cost, or consequence.

## Reject Rule

Mark `REVISE` if the hook only creates mood or curiosity without changing the protagonist's pressure.

## Repair Strategy

Tie the hook to at least one of:

- immediate danger;
- loss if ignored;
- active choice;
- social reaction;
- proof path;
- relationship shift;
- visible cost.

## Expected Decision

```text
REVISE
```
