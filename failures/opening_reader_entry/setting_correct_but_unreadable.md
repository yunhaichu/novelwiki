# Failure Case: Setting Correct But Unreadable

## Symptom

The opening accurately presents the planned setting, institution, world condition, or system context, but the target reader cannot enter the story.

## Reject Rule

Mark `REVISE` if setting correctness replaces reader entry.

A first-time reader must understand protagonist, immediate trouble, present desire, stake, and reason to continue before being asked to process heavy setting context.

## Repair Strategy

Convert setting into pressure.

Show one concrete rule, condition, deadline, object, or social reaction that forces the protagonist to choose, lose, hide, risk, or act.

Delay setting explanation that does not change the present scene.

## Expected Decision

```text
REVISE
```
