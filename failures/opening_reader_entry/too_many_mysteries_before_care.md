# Failure Case: Too Many Mysteries Before Care

## Symptom

The opening introduces several mysteries, secrets, abnormal signs, old cases, special terms, or hidden systems before the reader cares about the protagonist.

## Reject Rule

Mark `REVISE` if the reader must track multiple mysteries before understanding the immediate problem.

Mystery cannot replace protagonist desire, stake, and pressure.

## Repair Strategy

Keep one primary question in the opening.

Delay secondary mysteries until the reader understands:

- who the protagonist is;
- what they want now;
- what can be lost soon;
- why this story type is worth following.

## Expected Decision

```text
REVISE
```
