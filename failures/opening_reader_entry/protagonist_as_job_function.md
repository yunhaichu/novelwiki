# Failure Case: Protagonist As Job Function

## Symptom

The protagonist appears mainly as a worker, student, official, soldier, clerk, disciple, investigator, or process executor.

The reader sees what role the protagonist occupies, but not what the protagonist wants now.

## Reject Rule

Mark `REVISE` if the protagonist enters as a job function, setting observer, or plot camera rather than a person under pressure.

## Repair Strategy

Give the protagonist:

- an immediate desire;
- a present risk;
- one active choice;
- one visible cost or consequence.

The role may remain, but it must create pressure instead of replacing character.

## Expected Decision

```text
REVISE
```
